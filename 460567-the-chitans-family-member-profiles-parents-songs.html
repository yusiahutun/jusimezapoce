You might have heard of The Chitans, a family dedicated to winning souls to Jesus Christ through their music ministry. Their signing with Horizon records gave them a suitable platform to elevate their career. This family is indeed quite accomplished, and their fame is well-deserved. How much do you know about them?

The Chitans is an international gospel group consisting of five siblings, Kaiziah, Kayandra, Jesse, Jayden and Karlainah. For the past seven years, they have been doing full-time ministry with their parents moving to different countries across the world to spread the gospel through their music. Their ministry is based in Toronto, Canada.

The Chitans family member profiles

The Chitans is a family of five siblings from Toronto, Canada. Their mission is to minister the gospel through their music wherever they are called to. The five siblings were born and raised in Canada.

1. Kaiziah

She is the oldest among the five siblings. She was born on 26 November 1997. That means she is currently 24 years old. Her life mission is to be a missionary teacher and musician and to help the poor. She has adopted a child from El Salvador.

She is extremely skilful at composing music and poems. Additionally, she plays the piano, steel pan, alto saxophone, drums and violin.

2. Kayandra

She is the second born in her family. Kayandra is 23 years old as of 2022, as she was born on 1 June 1999. She intends to be a missionary doctor and professional musician. Just like her elder sister, Kaiziah, she has also adopted a child, Maritza, from Guatemala.

She is good at composing music and playing the piano. Additionally, she plays steel pan flute, drums and guitar.

3. Jesse

Jesse is the first son of Layne and Karen. He was born on 12 April 2001. That means he is currently 21 years old. He is looking forward to being a pastor and an electrical engineer. He likes playing the piano, steel pan drums, violin and bass guitar.

4. Jayden

Jayden is the second son and the fourth born of Layne and Karen. He was born on 1 December 2003. He is currently 18 years old. He is looking forward to being a police officer and a fireman. He plays steel pan. He is currently learning to play piano, alto sax and violin.

5. Karlainah

She is the last born in her family. Karlainah was born on 20 December, and she is 15 years old as of 2022. She wants to be a preacher and a doctor when she grows up. The young girl likes playing steel pan. She is currently learning to play the piano and violin.

The Chitans family worship

The five members developed a strong interest in music at a young age. They used to listen to music from the Gaither Vocal Band, which influenced their life for the better. Their music career began in family worship seven years ago, and since then, their career has continued to grow, aided mainly by the support they get from their parents.

Their music is centred on Southern Gospel, Contemporary Christian, Black Gospel, Caribbean, and traditional Hymns. They have caught the attention of numerous people with their smooth voices and well-thought-out lyrics, and they are often called upon to perform in different countries.

Even though they are young, they have organized several tours and concerts in various countries such as America, Canada, Europe, Africa and the Caribbean. In addition, they have been performing in churches, schools and cultural gatherings. They attended their first National Quartet Convention in 2017.

The Chitans have also adopted a school in Thika, Kenya, called The World Light School. They have already built new bathrooms and put in water tanks. They are currently constructing an administration block, kitchen and lunch room. Additionally, they plan to construct a computer and music room within the school.

The Chitans have also shared the stage with prominent gospel artists like Bill Gaither, Lynda Randle, Wintley Phipps, Jimmy Swaggart, Babbie Mason, Jonathan Nelson, Guy Penrod and many more.

Additionally, they have performed in prestigious venues and events, including Capitol Hill, The White House, NQC, and The Ark Encounter. They have also been to the Daystar television network and 3ABN television. Their debut professional CD was produced by Lynda Randle and recorded at Gaither's Studio.
