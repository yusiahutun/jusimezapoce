Short Bio

The American actor, Mike was born as Mike Randal Colter on 26 August 1976, in Columbia, South Carolina, USA. He holds an American nationality and belongs to African-American ethnicity. Jessica Jones star has a ripped body with a height of 1.91 m (6' 3''). He was born to parents Eddie Lee Colter Sr. and Freddie Marion Colter. He is the youngest of his five siblings and is the second cousin of Viola Davis. One of his siblings is a secret service agent. He resides in Los Angeles with his beautiful wife and daughter.

Mike graduated with a Bachelor's degree in theatre from the University of South Carolina. He completed his Masters in acting from Rutgers University.
