Meet Wylda Rae Johnson, Aaron and Sam Taylor-Johnson's Daughter

 Posted on April 18, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  737 words  |  Martina Birk

Wylda Rae Johnson was born into fame because of who her parents are. Aaron and Sam Taylor-Johnson are famous for how well they’ve done in their respective careers and their age gap. 

Aaron Taylor-Johnson is a famous actor who has appeared in several blockbusters, including “Kick-Ass,” “Savages,” and “Avengers: Age of Ultron.” Sam Taylor-Johnson is a filmmaker and photographer. 

Some movies she has directed include “Nowhere Boy,” and “Fifty Shades of Grey.” Wylda is the first child Aaron and Sam welcomed together as a couple. 

They have one more daughter, Romy Hero Johnson. They also raise Angelica Jopling and Jessie Phoenix Jopling, Sam’s children from her first marriage to art dealer Jay Jopling. 

Wylda Rae Johnson was born on July 7, 2010, in London. Her father was 20 years old when she was born, while Sam was 43 years old. 

Becoming a parent at such a young age is not an easy task since there is still a lot of growing up to do as an individual. However, Aaron seems to have been born with an all-you-need-to-know manual because he excels at it. 

Aaron is a great father and enjoys reading to Wylda. The proud dad and his wife, who is 23 years older than him, are huge on privacy where their daughter is concerned.

Some believe this has to do with some people’s negative reviews about their relationships. Wylda rarely attends events with her parents, and they hardly publicly talk about her or her sister. 

The same thing goes for social media. Sam and Aaron avoid sharing pictures of their kids; on the rare occasions they post about them, their faces never show in the photos. For instance, in 2020, Wylda broke her arm, and Sam shared the news on her Instagram page. Wylda’s face was hidden in the post, but her injured arm was evident. 

Wylda Rae Johnson’s Siblings

Wylda has three siblings; her sister Romy and her two half-sisters, Angelica and Jessie. The pair were born while Sam was with Jopling. Wylda’s oldest sister Angelica was born on April 24, 1997. The next most senior is Jessie, who was born in November 2005. The youngest is Romy, who was born on January 18, 2012. 

Like her biological father, Wylda’s older sister, Angelica, grew up loving art. She is now the founding director of an exhibition program called “Incubator.” Aaron raised all four girls with love even though he became a parent at a young age (he is seven years older than Angelica). 

Details on Sam and Aaron’s Romance

Sam and Aaron have been married for over ten years. They met in 2008 when Aaron auditioned for the lead role in Sam’s debut project as a director, “Nowhere Boy.” 

One thing led to another, and they started dating when Aaron was 19. Aaron asked the big question one year after that meeting, and Sam said yes. Their relationship made many headlines in its infancy because of their age gap. 

Sam took a lot of heat because of the age gap, but the director could care less. Speaking about how their union works, she once said: 

“I don’t really analyze our relationship. I just know that it works.”

She also added: “I just feel secure and loved and safe. We have this profound connection. We’re just in sync.”

Sam and Aaron took their time before they tied the knot. Aaron settled in as a father to her two kids and became a biological dad after Wylda and Romy came along. 

The famous actor and Sam eventually married two years after their two biological children were born. The date was June 21, 2012, and it bound the two for what is now looking like forever. 

The Couple Renewed Their Vows

Despite the backlash they have gotten, age has been and will forever remain just a number to the couple. They renewed their vows in June 2022. The actor posted about it on his Instagram page.

He shared a lovely picture of him and Sam. He captioned it: “Yesterday was the most beautiful day, summer solstice, our 10th anniversary… we renewed our vows to one another and confessed our love in front of our nearest and dearest friends and family; it was a celebration of love and joy!”

Sam and Aaron are not the only celebrity couple with a decades-long age gap between them. Others include Mila Kunis and Ashton Kutcher, George and Amal Clooney, and Harrison Ford and Calista Flockhart.
