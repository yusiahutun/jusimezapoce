 Posted on September 4, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  404 words  |  Aldo Pusey
  • A viral video captured a construction worker carrying a 50-kilogram bag of cement using only his teeth
  • The man in the clip rocked a black overall and ascended a staircase, leaving onlookers astounded
  • The video prompted a wave of surprise and amazement from netizens, who flooded the comment section to share their opinions

A man has stunned many people on social media with his incredible feat of carrying a 50-kilogram bag of cement with his teeth.

The video of the man was posted on X by influencer @omwamba, who asked his followers if this was talent or not.

"Is this Talent or...?" Omwamba wondered.

Jaw-dropping staircase escapade

The video depicts the man wearing a black overall and carrying the heavy bag with his teeth as he climbs up a staircase at a construction site.

Separately, a hard-working man has disclosed that he receives a KSh 8,800 monthly salary for carrying 300 bags of cement daily.

In a video on Twitter, the man said that he works with another person in the company, and each person is required to carry 300 bags of cement daily.

"Everybody carries 300 bags a day. We are two people, so 600 bags. They give us KSh 8,800 a month. I am telling the truth."

When the person recording the video asked him how many bags he would carry when he got the opportunity to travel abroad, the man said he would even work harder since that would earn him more money.
