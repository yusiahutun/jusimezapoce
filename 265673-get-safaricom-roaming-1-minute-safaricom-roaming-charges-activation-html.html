Get Safaricom roaming in 1 minute - Safaricom roaming charges and activation

 Posted on March 13, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  559 words  |  Martina Birk

Safaricom roaming is the service that allows you to use your local number in different parts of the world. The goal is to ensure that you access the same services that you subscribe to at home when you are abroad or in a different geographic range. Customers get to communicate without the hustle of adjusting to the bundles of the service providers in other countries through the Safaricom International Roaming Services. The main bundles that you can access through roaming are SMS, voice, and data services. Another advantage is that Safaricom roaming services are available to both PostPay and PrePay customers.

Safaricom Roaming Activation

Activating Safaricom roaming services is easy. Visit a Safaricom retailer for both prepaid and postpaid activation services.

Alternatively, you can send an email to advantage@safaricom.co.ke to make the activation request.

Once activated, you can begin to receive and send messages and make calls using the roaming bundles. The tip for prepaid users is to make a deposit on airtime or carry extra scratch cards when travelling. PostPay users must also deposit Ksh.5000 when requesting to activate roaming services.

Your phone will automatically search for service providers that are in partnership with Safaricom on the roaming services (on prepay lines). You will then select your preferred network and enjoy the Safaricom roaming services.

How to Call and Text while Roaming Internationally

You can make phone calls to any region within the country you are visiting by dialling the area code followed by the phone number.

Making calls to Kenya is also that simple: Dial the country code (+ 254) followed by the number you wish to call, e.g., dial +254 7xx xxxxxx for Safaricom mobile users, and dial +254 20 xxxxxx for Nairobi landline.

To call other countries, simply dial + country code, area code, number, e.g., to call the UK, dial + 4477 xxxxxxxxx.

To send SMS to Kenya or to an international number, type your message and send to the number as above

Safaricom roaming charges vary depending on the region. East African roaming rates that apply to Uganda, Rwanda and South Sudan are only Ksh.10.

The international calling bundles offer cheaper rates but only apply to calls initiated from Kenya. Calling zone 1 countries like Canada, the U.S, and China goes for as low as Ksh.2.50. To subscribe to these bundles, dial *100# for prepay users and *200# for postpay subscribers.

You can use Okoa Jahazi credit to make international calls. Also, your Bonga points will accumulate depending on the amount of airtime you use.

Safaricom Roaming Data Bundles

The Safaricom international roaming services also allow you to buy data bundles at a cheap rate in different countries. Safaricom recently slashed the data rates in over 50 countries for over 200 networks. The charges now go for as low as Ksh.14 per MB.
