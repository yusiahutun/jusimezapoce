Emily Ratajkowski Wears a Brown Naked Dress with an Exposed Thong

 Posted on June 10, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  387 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Emily Ratajkowski has taken her sultry style to the streets and gardens of France.

The model is currently enjoying a relaxing holiday with her son, Sylvester, and has been sharing photos from their trip on social media. Today, she posted a slideshow which included photos with her baby boy, snaps of delicious meals, romantic sunsets, and dreamy landscapes, as well as photos of her recent vacation outfits.

In one photo, EmRata is seen standing in what looks to be a church cemetery wearing a frilly sheer brown dress. The completely see-through piece features spaghetti straps and breezy layers. The My Body author skipped the bra, wearing the midi dress only with a black thong which was visible under the thin sheer fabric. Ratajkowski finished the summer look with strappy light-brown sandals, a brown leather shoulder bag, and skinny black sunglasses.

In another slide, Ratajkowski stands in a balcony and wears a silky ivory slipdress with an open back featuring handkerchief-like ties in a subtle pink floral print. She paired it with strappy golden heeled sandals. In the photo, little Sly is seen playing with a blue toy car beside his mom; he looks adorable in a yellow T-shirt and white-and-yellow checkered shorts.

"Sly can say 'merci' now," Ratajkowski simply captioned the post, which has since been deleted.
