Inspired by Ben's love for sketching his furniture ideas on the spot and in the field, we've created this beautiful leather companion for your pocket and biggest ideas. 

"All of our products are made from full-grain leather that is tanned in the most respected tanneries around the world that are sourced from the highest quality bovine, equine, and bison. We hand-stitch every product using a traditional technique called saddle stitching, which uses two threads for maximum strength to ensure that your products withstand the daily wear and tear for many years to come. Our products are stitched using Ritza 25 (tiger thread) and thread from the Maine Thread Company, both of which are regarded as some of the most durable and best hand-stitching thread by many professionals in the craft."
