Watch the Second Installment of Pierre Balmain's Video Series

 Posted on July 4, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  172 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Model Abbey Lee Kershaw and her Our Mountain bandmate/real-life boyfriend Matthew Hutchinson run through the streets of New York and Brooklyn in the second installment of the Pierre Balmain video series, directed by Inez and Vinoodh. Set to their hit "Devil Banquet" Lee steals the show with her tambourine playing skills and good looks, much like she did in the first video, but the band boys find their way into the spotlight, showing off a preview of Pierre Balmain menswear (both the men's and women's collection will debut in Paris this weekend). Watch the clip above.
