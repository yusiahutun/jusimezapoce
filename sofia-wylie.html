Professional Life and Career

Dancing Career

Having an interest in dance, Sofia started her dance career, participating in the Television dance competition show  So You Think You Can Dance in 2011. Similarly, she also appeared in America’s Got Talent as a dancer in 2015. Likewise, she also performed on Justine Bieber’s Purpose World Tour. Sofia began the Internet-based 4K Dance Series in 2017 and an IGTV dance series, Dancing with Sofia Wylie in 2019.

Acting Career

Sofia started her acting career with the role of June in the TV series “Nicky, Ricky, Dicky, & Dawn” in 2017. After that, she appeared in other TV short films like “Disney Channel Stars: DuckTales Theme Song” (2017), “Disney Channel Stars: Legendary” (2018), and “Marvel Rising: Heart of Iron” (2019).

In 2017, Sofia got her breakthrough with her role as Buffy Driscoll in the Disney TV series “Andi Mack”. She appeared in 57 episodes of the series from 2017 to 2019. Her performance was hugely appreciated by the audience. Moreover, her other famous TV series include “Shook” (2019),  “Spider-Man: Maximum Venom” (2020), “High School Musical: The Musical – The Series” (2019-2021), and many more.

Awards
