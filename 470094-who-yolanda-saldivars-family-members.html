Who is Yolanda Saldivar?

Yolanda is an American former nurse. She was convicted of the murder of Selena in 1995. She was born on September 19th, 1960, in San Antonio, Texas, United States.

Yolanda attended three different elementary schools. She first attended Kennedy High School, then moved to Jay High School before graduating from McCollom High School. In 1985, she was accepted at the University of Texas. She later transferred to Palo Alto College, where she graduated as a registered nurse.

What happened between Yolanda and Selena?

Selena and her father suspected Yolanda of embezzling money. Fans had begun calling and sending frustrating letters to the Quintanilla family. They claimed they hadn’t received the merchandise they had ordered and paid for.
