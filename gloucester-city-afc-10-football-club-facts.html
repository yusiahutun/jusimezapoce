Gloucester City AFC has a rich history dating back to 1883

In 2007, Gloucester City AFC faced a devastating setback when their stadium, Meadow Park, was completely submerged during widespread flooding. It took years of dedication and hard work to rebuild the club and restore their facilities, showcasing the resilience and determination of the team.
