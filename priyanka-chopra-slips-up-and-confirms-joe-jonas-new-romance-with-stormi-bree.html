Joe Jonas and Stormi Bree have been rumored to be dating for a few weeks now, and Priyanka Chopra may have just confirmed it, as they seemingly head on a double date with her and Nick.

The ‘rumored’ celebrity couple comes just months after Joe Jonas‘ shock divorce from Game Of Thrones star Sophie Turner. It looks like she’s also moved on, so for those who were hoping for a reunion, we don’t think that’s going to happen anytime soon. Especially now The Jo Bro’ may be introducing his ‘new flame’ to the family…

Priyanka Chopra ‘breaks cover’ on double date with Joe Jonas and Stormi Bree

Oh, the joys of Instagram. Priyanka innocently posted a photo of four hands clinking champagne glasses together, but it seemed like the internet detectives were on it, causing her to swiftly delete the post from her stories.

One hand in the picture seemingly had the same tattoo as Joe’s rumored new boo has on hers, and they then put two and two together.

The speculation is that Joe and Stormi joined Nick Jonas and Priyanka Chopra on a double date. It must be ‘getting serious’ if she’s hanging out with Joe’s family, but it looks like they don’t want fans to know that just yet as the post was swiftly deleted.

Joe, 34, and Stormi, 33 sent the internet into a frenzy when they were spotted on a ‘romantic’ trip to Aspen together.

It’s not the first time Priyanka and the model have interacted either, as once again, internet detectives discovered they recently started following each other on the ‘gram.

Despite their best efforts, it seems like they’re not fooling anyone, as one user wrote: “I mean worst kept secret ever atp they should just let it be but it’s funny to watch them all try to sneaky sneak.”

Sophie Turner is ‘relieved’ to see Joe moving on

Despite being married for four years, and having two daughters together, it’s reported that Sophie Turner is ‘relieved’ to see her ex ‘moving on’.

“She’s not bothered with who Joe is seeing. She’s relieved to see him moving on,” a source told OK! Magazine.

The 27-year-old herself has seemingly moved on, with British aristocrat, Peregrine Pearson. The pair have been seen engaging in PDA with each other on several occasions, most recently, wandering around London with their arms around each other last week.
