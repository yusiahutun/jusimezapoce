The Crown's Meg Bellamy joined co-star Ed McVey at the press night performance of Stranger Things: The First Shadow in London on Thursday.

The actress, 21, who plays Kate Middleton in the Netflix series, put on a leggy display  in a chic leather mini skirt as she walked the red carpet.

Meg also donned a matching trench coach which she teamed over an asymmetrical top and stylish ankle boots.

Meanwhile Prince William actor Ed, 24, cut an equally stylish figure in a green pinstriped jacket and cream wide leg trousers.

He also donned a white vest to complete the ensemble which teased a glimpse of his hairy chest and muscular physique.

It comes after Meg admitted that she 'didn't remember the real life events' that are being depicted in the final season as the trio revealed how that impacted their performances. 

The final six episodes of The Crown, which were released on Netflix on December 14, bring the key moment at university in the blossoming relationship between the now-Prince and Princess of Wales onto our screens. 

During an appearance on This Morning Meg explained: 'We were babies we don't really remember anything from the actual real life events of this series.'

'I think that is helpful because we were able to just look at it as a character on the page,' Ed added. 

Recalling the moment they discovered they had secured their roles, co-star Luther Ford who plays Prince Harry recalled: 'It was an open casting call and my sister sent it me. I was studying film at the time and I had no plans to become an actor.

'My mum specifically told me, you're never going to get the part you look nothing like him!' 

Meg explained: 'I was at Legoland Windsor when I got the call. I was playing a giant snake when I got the message. 

'I had to run into a room and when I came back out the first person I spoke to was a customer who was like, "my queue skip card isn't working" I was like "hang on a minute" because I thought I might pass out.'

This week, Netflix teased fans with a sneak peek of the moment Kate Middleton first caught the eye of Prince William at a St Andrew's charity fashion show back in 2002.

Wokingham Actress Meg, who was plucked from obscurity to play the role, struts down the runway at St Andrews' Bay Hotel in the famous sheer dress and black underwear. 

The teaser clip shows a young William dazzled by his future wife - and the moment the pair first lock eyes. 

It begins with cheering and screaming from the audience, before the camera pans up and we see Kate in the Charlotte Todd-designed strapless dress with turquoise trim. Kate, whose tresses fall in the same ringlet waves as the princess's 20 years prior, then begins to strut down the catwalk and the shot switches to Prince William - who looks up from his glass of wine.

It is said to be the turning point in the couple's relationship, as they eventually went from close friends to developing romantic feelings for each other.
