Meaning of Angel by Akon

 Posted on May 10, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  529 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

The song "Angel" by Akon is a beautiful and soulful track that touches upon the theme of finding hope and inspiration in the most unexpected places. Through the lyrics, Akon invites listeners on a journey of self-discovery and encourages them to embrace the divine energy that exists within and around them.One standout lyric in the song is "I'm looking at an angel, and believe me when I say she got that whole place glowing." This lyric not only captures the essence of the song but also introduces the idea of seeing beauty and goodness in others. It suggests that by recognizing and appreciating the positive qualities in people, we can bring light and happiness to our own lives and the world around us.Another lyric that stands out is "She got wings, she got a halo, it seems to me so unnatural." This line evokes imagery associated with angels and further emphasizes the theme of spirituality present throughout the song. It explores the idea that encountering someone who possesses qualities typically associated with celestial beings can feel extraordinary and even surreal. The lyric encourages listeners to embrace and celebrate the extraordinary qualities they may find in themselves and others.The line "She looked at me, took me by surprise yeah, as if she took me by the hand to some foreign land, and had me up way above the clouds in the sky singing" adds a sense of wonder and awe to the song. It suggests that encountering this angelic figure has a profound impact on the narrator, transporting them to a higher state of being. This theme resonates with the idea of finding inspiration and guidance from unexpected sources, as well as the potential for personal transformation.Akon's repeated use of the phrase "way above the clouds in the sky singing" serves as a powerful mantra throughout the song. It not only conveys a sense of liberation and freedom but also alludes to the notion that music and song can transport us to another realm. The act of singing becomes a metaphor for expressing one's truest self, unencumbered by worldly constraints. It signifies the importance of embracing individuality and finding solace in creative expression.The closing lyrics of the song, "Singing my song from dusk till dawn, I know you got a lot on your mind but it won't be long" offer a message of hope and resilience. It acknowledges the challenges and burdens that we all carry but reassures listeners that brighter days are on the horizon. The repetition of the phrase "singing my song" implies that each individual has a unique story to tell and a voice that deserves to be heard.In conclusion, the song "Angel" by Akon delves into the themes of finding inspiration, embracing the extraordinary, and seeking solace in music. Through its imaginative lyrics, the song encourages listeners to recognize and appreciate the beauty and goodness in others. It urges them to express their authentic selves, embrace their potential for transformation, and find hope in the face of adversity. Ultimately, "Angel" serves as a reminder of the power we all possess to create our own moments of transcendence and to become angels in our own right.
