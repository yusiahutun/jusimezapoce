Bongo star Diamond Platnumz has shown off the stunning home he and his team are occupying during his US music tour.

The singer recently began his 'Diamond Platnumz US Tour 2021' and is set to perform in different states in the month of October.

The Jeje hitmaker went on his Instagram Stories to share a video of the stunning villa he will be calling home.

The house is located in Los Angeles and has a lovely white interior that anyone would admire.

One could spot his beloved cousin Romy Jones sharing a good laugh with one of the singer's dancers in a small living room.

On the other side of the said room was a large dining area and an even bigger sitting area beside it.

The singer also gave fans a nice view of the backyard with a large and luxurious swimming pool that they will certainly relish.

US tour details

Diamond's tour kicked off in Washington DC on Sunday, October 10, and will visit several other US states in the coming days.

His next stop will be in Minneapolis on October 15, and after that, he will perform in other states such as Denver, New York, Arizona, Texas, among others.

He got a great reception with his first performance, something that he will be hoping to get in the other areas considering his popularity.

The renowned Tanzanian singer recently encouraged his social media followers as she shared a throwback video of when he was starting out as a Bongo artiste.

Diamond shared a video of his November 2013 release Kamwambie, captioned with a message of encouragement.

In the video, Diamond looked scrawny as he belted out the tune, which became a big hit in East Africa.

Arguably the regions’ biggest star at the moment, Diamond, who has now achieved success beyond music, urged his followers not to give up.

He told his fans to watch the Kamwambie video whenever they felt like throwing in the towel, hoping that it would encourage them that better days in the offing.
