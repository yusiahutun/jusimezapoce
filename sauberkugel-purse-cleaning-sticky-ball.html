Of course, after the ball does its job, you may be wondering how to get all those crumbs and pieces of lint off of it. The good news is that The Clean Ball is fully reusable, and all you need to do is rinse it off before slipping it back into your bag.

“Drop in your purse or bag and forget about it,” says one customer review. “Take out every few days and rinse under water and dry with paper towel. Has helped me keep my purse cleaner than ever and without wasting time cleaning the inside with wipes that takes forever.”

While you might have to explain what, exactly, this little ball does, it’s a great addition to a larger gift or even a Secret Santa exchange where you have to buy something on a budget. The Clean Ball comes in multiple colors and is perfect for keeping in purses and beyond. Someone who lugs around a gym bag, carries a backpack to school, is constantly traveling with a suitcase, or recently added a diaper bag into the mix will all be able to enjoy this accessory.
