Lady Gaga &amp; Ariana Grande Developed Sisterly Bond on &quot;Rain on Me&quot;

 Posted on May 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  299 words  |  Martina Birk
  • Lady Gaga and Ariana Grande were "like sisters" during the making of "Rain on Me," according to Gaga's choreographer, Richy Jackson.
  • Jackson told People, Gaga and Grande "were laughing, cracking up, speaking about fashion, going over the dance moves, talking to each other."
  • According to the choreographer, Grande "was really just free to come into the House of Gaga and perform and give her best."

Most people will have heard Lady Gaga and Ariana Grande's epic collaboration, "Rain on Me," by now. And in a completely unsurprising turn of events, it's being reported that the two superstars created literal magic during rehearsals for the "Rain on Me" music video.

Speaking to People, Lady Gaga's longtime choreographer, Richy Jackson, revealed that the two singers were "like sisters" during the music video shoot. According to Jackson, Grande easily fit into Gaga's team, as the choreographer explained, "Ari allowed herself to come into our world."

Per Jackson, Grande "spoke to Gaga about how Gaga saw the video, how she saw the fashion, the hair, the makeup, the dancing. ... She was really just free to come into the House of Gaga and perform and give her best, through our eye and through our lens and through our choreography."

After describing Gaga and Grande as "like sisters," Jackson revealed that the pair "were laughing, cracking up, speaking about fashion, going over the dance moves, talking to each other, coordinating with each other." He continued, "It's always hard work. ... Though we were working, it was still such a good time."
