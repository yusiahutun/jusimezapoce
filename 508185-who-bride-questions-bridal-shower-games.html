Are you planning a bridal shower but wondering how to spice up the night for the ladies? One way to put smiles on your guests' faces and have an epic bridal shower is through playing games such as "who knows the bride best". A bridal shower is never complete without exciting games to entertain the guests. Planning for some wedding shower questions is a guaranteed crowd-pleaser and a brilliant way to break the ice while putting the bride-to-be on the spot before the big day.
