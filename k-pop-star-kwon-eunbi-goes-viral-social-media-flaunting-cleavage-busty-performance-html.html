A popular K-pop idol from South Korea has gone viral following her performance at a music festival.

Kwon Eunbi, 27, has been trending both at home and abroad after flaunting her cleavage on stage at the The Cass Cool Festival in Gwacheol last week.

Footage of the performance was uploaded to YouTube and quickly racked up 600,000 views in just two days.

Clips, photos, and GIFs of the event have since made their way to social media platforms like Instagram and X (formerly known as Twitter), where views are in the millions.

In the performance, the stunning singer shows off her curves in a plunging blue midriff top and matching miniskirt.

Another gushed, 'She truly is a goddess.'

She later embarked on a solo career, which had little success until recently when she started to go viral on social media for her impressive physique.

The 27-year-old started to trend after performing in skimpy attire at one of Korea's annual Waterbomb summer music festivals.

At Waterbomb, artists perform while fans in the crowd squirt them with water pistols.

After her first performance at the festival went viral, Eunbi has followed it up with more racy summer concerts.

Her songs have now started to shoot up the charts and she's already booked a big endorsement deal with a Korean skincare brand.

During an appearance on the variety show Strong Heart League, Eunbi admitted that she'd become a hot property in the entertainment industry after performing at Waterbomb.

'I've been receiving a lot of calls from people around me these days,' she said. 'They should have contacted me before!'
