Rihanna Purple Princess Dress - Rihanna Princess Dress at London Fashion Week

 Posted on April 10, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  170 words  |  Larita Shotwell

BadGal RiRi channeled her inner princess once again. The singer-turned-beauty mogul attended her Fenty Beauty launch event in London looking like a fairytale with a lilac tulle dress by Molly Goddard, Chopard jewels, Anita Ko ear cuffs, and metallic sandals by Rene Caovilla that wrapped up to her calves.

Getty Images

Rihanna kept her waves in a high ponytail and sported purple highlighter and eyeshadow—courtesy of her own line—to go along with her outfit. She even wrapped up with a matching shawl, as if the ensemble wasn't regal enough.

Getty Images

After sporting everything from a dramatic high-low gown to her own motocross-inspired Fenty x Puma collection during NYFW, Rihanna continues to show her fashion versatility in London with this frilled and voluminous princess-esque number.

Keep slaying, RiRi.
