Coronation Street Kylie Platt star's hottest snaps - Madonna vibes to gym workout

 Posted on September 1, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  774 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Coronation Street fans recently went wild as former Kylie Platt star Paula Lane sharing a fresh-faced snap on Instagram this week.

The actress looked ultra glam while enjoying some time out at Manchester Christmas markets - and it is not the first glamorous look that the star has been pictured in.

Throughout the past decade, Paula has wowed fans with some seriously sexy outfits on the red carpet, as well as delighting audiences with beautiful theatre costumes and gym workout shoots.

Gym glamour

As part of an interview for The Mirror's Take It Easy Magazine, Paula flaunted her toned figure in a gym inspired photo shoot.

The actress' washboard abs and toned arms were on show beneath a grey sports bra and white gillet, with the star looking radiant as she flaunted her sunkissed skin.

Paula finished the look with a pair of tiny gym shorts, with the outfit complimented by the star's gorgeous long brown locks flowed past her shoulders in the snap.

Madonna vibes

Paula gave fans Madonna vibes as she donned one of the music megastar's famous early eighties looks for a sultry snap.

The star's jaw-dropping look included a white lace dress, with the busty, lingerie style top flaunting her tiny waist and fabulous figure.

Matched with white lace gloves, strings of pearls and a large lace bow, as well as lashings of mascara and eye-liner, Paula sizzled in the stunning snap.

Red carpet chic

Paula is no stranger to a red carpet look or two, with the star gracing many awards ceremonies during her time on Coronation Street.

She looked sensational as she smiled for the cameras at the National Television Awards 2014 while wearing a red sheer lace dress, which flowed over the star's beautiful curves.

Adding a pop of red lipstick and wearing an up-do hairstyle, Paula oozed high-fashion glamour as she turned heads on the night.

Pretty in pink

Since leaving Coronation Street, Paula has continued to delight fans in TV series including Call The Midwife, as well as musicals such as Kinky Boots.

The star shared a backstage snap of her and a fellow Kinky Boots co-star on Instagram, where Paula looked fabulous wearing a bright pink a-line dress.

Flaunting her endless legs, which were adorned with bright red heels, Paula looked beautiful as she smiled for the camera in the sweet snap.

Soap icon

Paula is undoubtedly a soap icon, and she looked every inch the red carpet icon too, when she arrived at the BAFTA TV Awards 2020 in a dazzling black sequin gown.

The top sparkled with the sequins, while the floor length black skirt highlighted her gorgeous figure.

Giving us all Audrey Hepburn vibes, Paula looked beautiful as she complimented the look with smoky eyes, red lipstick and her brunette locks tied in a bun.

Summer vibes

Paula looks as radiant away from our screens, as she does on them, as she relaxed at home in a sizzling summer snap.

The actress wore a beautiful white summer dress decorated with embroidered flowers, with the plunging dress giving a cheeky flash of skin underneath.

Wearing a barely there make-up look, Paula gave us all fashion envy with her effortless chic style.

Awards glamour

Paula looked sensational as she arrived at the British Soap Awards 2013 in a stunning electric blue gown.

The floor length dress featured a cut out back, which flaunted the star's toned figure, with the off the shoulder look becoming one of our favourite looks from the star yet.

Paired with a half up-half down hairstyle and a white clutch bag, Paula enchanted the crowds with her incredible style.
