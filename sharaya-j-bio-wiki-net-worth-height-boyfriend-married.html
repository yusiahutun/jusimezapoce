Sharaya J’s Bio, Wiki

Sharaya J was born Sharaya Howell on 17 January in 1984 in Honolulu, Hawaii. As of 2018, she is 34 years old. She was born under the birth sign Capricorn. Her father served as one-fifth of the hip-hop group Double XX Posse. She was raised in Jersey City, New Jersey.

Sharaya however, started her career as a hip-hop choreographer. She first featured with the French singer Mylene Farmer‘s 2006 tour Avant que l’ombre… à Bercy. Additionally, her work caught the attention of famous musicians which includes Ciara, Alicia Keys, Rihanna, Diddy and Jay-Z.  Moreover, she got a chance to work on each artists’ music video.

However, her music career began in January 2010 when she signed Missy Elliott’s label The Goldmind Inc. On 2 March 2013 Sharaya release her single BANJI through iTunes. In June 2014, J previewed her forthcoming single Shut It Down. as of 2018, she joined the music competition program named The Four: Battle for Stardom. In the program, she attained the title for the longest seat holder and runner-up of the second season.
