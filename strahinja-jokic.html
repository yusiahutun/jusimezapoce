Nikola Jokic

At an early age, his brother introduced Nickola to the sport and he fell in love with it. Jokić played youth basketball for Vojvodina Srbijagas.

He caught the eye of bigger clubs and joined Mega Basket in the summer of 2012. After playing for two seasons, he registered for the 2014 NBA Draft and was chosen 41st overall.
