'House of Payne' Season 9 Episode 4: Jasmine gets grilled about her virginity, disgusted fans call i

 Posted on July 24, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  461 words  |  Aldo Pusey

In Episode 3, we saw Malik (Doc Shaw) finally reveal to his parents CJ and Janine that his stripper girlfriend Lisa is pregnant. In Episode 4, Jasmine wheedles the truth out of Ella about Malik, using an expert form of manipulation. However, while this drags on for half the episode and the last 10 minutes are spent in Ella and Janine grilling Jasmine about her sexual endeavours if she has any.

We don't know the truth yet because there seems to be something that Jasmine is clearly hiding. Is she seeing the boy at church or is she being shamed for being a virgin? Fans are putting their heads together and trying to unravel the truth. In the last few minutes of the episode, there is a rather somber silence as compared to the normal cheery tone of the show. Ella tells Janine to give it a rest and not ask Jasmine any more questions for now. So perhaps, we'll find out the truth soon, if there is anything, that is.

"Drilling Jasmine over and over about her virginity status?? That was Hella cringy. #HouseOfPayne," a fan tweeted.

"I don’t like how Jasmine literally told them multiple times that she was a virgin yet they just spent 10 minutes asking her over and over again if she was having sex when Malik is the one who’s about to have a baby yet they didn’t have the same energy for him #HouseOfPayne," another wrote.
