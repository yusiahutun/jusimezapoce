James Maddison's FPL team gives huge Tottenham squad hint ahead of Burnley clash

 Posted on February 8, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  452 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

James Maddison's Fantasy Premier League team may have leaked a change to Tottenham's line-up against Burnley.

The England international, who has hit the ground running in north London, has put one of his team-mates on the bench while drafting another in to the starting XI before their trip to Turf Moor.

Richarlison is the man to drop out Wirth defender Destiny Udogie coming in following his fine start to life under Ange Postecoglou.

FPL players will be delighted that the late change doesn't relate to Maddison himself, with 23% of players having the 26-year-old in their squads ahead of the fourth match of the season.
