NeNe Leakes' Unloads on Shere Whitfield for 'Mugshot' Diss on 'RHOA'

 Posted on July 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  273 words  |  Martina Birk

On Sunday's episode of "The Real Housewives of Atlanta," Shereé Whitfield threw some shade at NeNe Leakes after the latter said Sheree's incarcerated boyfriend, Tyrone, was a "con artist" -- and the comment did not sit well with NeNe.

"Really, NeNe? You and Gregg both have mugshots," Shereé said during her confessional. Following the episode's airing, NeNe went on a full-blown rant about the comment on "Watch What Happens Live" Sunday night.

"I really don't care about Shereé bringing up a mugshot," NeNe told Andy Cohen. "Shereé is trying to -- a mugshot does not equal -- anybody can get a mugshot, first of all, for a traffic ticket."

"A mugshot does not equal 10 years in prison for doing a Ponzi scheme," NeNe continued. "I ain't spent a day in jail doing nothing, honey, OK? So you got 10 years in prison, you sure won't walk around here being nice to no damn body. You got 10 years with the feds, honey. That's a whole other situation."

"And by the way, Shereé need to worry about her own mugshot. Her son got a mugshot and her man got a mugshot, so please don't call out no mugshots," NeNe added. "Let's be for real. She had hers expunged. She was stealing out the store, remember?"

In case you're wondering, NeNe used the word "mugshot" eight times in 40 seconds.

"The Real Housewives of Atlanta" airs Sunday nights on Bravo.
