I Walked for Miles in These Comfy Suede Booties on First Wear

 Posted on June 10, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  486 words  |  Martina Birk

I can’t deny it: I love a sleek bootie. Nothing gets me giddier than curating a neutral fall uniform consisting of a midi skirt, turtleneck, and blazer, topped with this autumnal shoe. The footwear style is omnipresent in almost all of my seasonal looks, and owning one that’s supportive, comfortable, and durable is top of mind when it comes to investing in a new pair.

My most recent shoe addition is the Nisolo Ana Go-To Heeled Chelsea Boot in taupe suede. I can confidently say I’ve never loved a pair of shoes more, and right now, they’re 30 percent off through September 19, along with the rest of Nisolo’s site with code FALL30. But run, since deals like these don’t happen often. 

Nisolo Ana Go-To Heeled Chelsea Boot

When I received the shoes to test, I performed the bravest task of all — wearing them straight out of the box and walking for miles on the unbalanced streets of New York City. But after wearing Nisolo’s Ana Go-To Heeled Chelsea Boots for hours, I instantly fell in love with them. They’re made with high-quality, breathable, and water-resistant leather (a must for New York’s unpredictable weather) and feature cushioned five-layer memory foam insoles that feel like walking on clouds. The 2.5-inch heel offered ample height but didn’t feel difficult to walk in. You can also snag the sister shoe, the Heeled Chelsea Boot, which is double-discounted during the sale. 

After wearing them for three consecutive hours, I had zero pain or blisters and even felt confident enough that I could literally run in them. I styled them with a simple athletic dress and white button-down top, and they pulled the casual look together with an elevated touch. Their round-toe shape was comfortable and didn’t pinch my feet, and the slip-resistant rubber heel caps felt sturdy even while pounding the New York City pavement that’s oftentimes cracked, uneven, and wet. The Chelsea boot is available in four fall-ready colors, including taupe suede, almond, auburn, and black — the latter three of which have a smooth, shiny finish.

One of my favorite features of the stylish heels is that they’re sustainably made, and every Nisolo purchase ensures a living wage for those working on the shoes from start to finish. Even better, the brand offers a recycling program through Soles4Souls, where you can receive credit towards a new pair and donate your old ones to micro-entrepreneurs in need instead of contributing to excessive landfill waste.

If you also look forward to wearing booties throughout fall, don’t pass up the Ana Go-To Chelsea Boots from Nisolo while the entire site is 30 percent off with code FALL30. And keep scrolling for other styles on sale for two more days.
