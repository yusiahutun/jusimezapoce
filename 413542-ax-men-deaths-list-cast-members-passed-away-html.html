Why did Ax Men get cancelled?

The show was cancelled in 2019 after thirteen seasons, reportedly due to declining ratings and high production costs. However, the History Channel has not provided an official statement regarding the cancellation.

What happened to Rygaard logging crew?

In 2016, the crew suffered a tragic loss when their owner and leader, Craig Rygaard, was killed in a car accident. The crew continued to operate under the leadership of Craig's brother, Jason, but they eventually went out of business in 2018 due to financial difficulties
