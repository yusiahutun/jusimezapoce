Wiz Khalifa DYES his dreadlocks bright purple

 Posted on February 22, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  619 words  |  Larita Shotwell

As his soon-to-be wife poured her heart out on social media on Wiz Khalifa decided to distract himself from the drama another way.

The 27-year-old spent most of the early hours of Tuesday morning bleaching and then dyeing his dreadlocks a bright shade of purple.

The rapper posted a series of photographs as his black hair was transformed by barber Tracy Love.

Dubbing the finished look 'Purple Rain' after the song by Prince, the star seemed pretty happy with the results making the snap of the final product his Twitter profile picture.

As Wiz was using his social media to make fashion statement, his wife of just 14 months used hers to pour her heart out.

Amber took to Twitter Monday to explain how she is feeling since she filed divorce paper's against the father of her child late last month.

Father son day: Wiz's wife Amber Rose is petitioning for full custody of their son Sebastian, but the rapper and the toddler got some time together in Los Angeles, California, on Sunday

Despite whispers the former stripper had moved on with both Nick Cannon and Nick Simmons, it appears she is still struggling to come to the grips with breaking up with the father of her child.

In her divorce filing, Amber has petitioned for full custody of Sebastian with Wiz to have visitation rights. Wiz has fought back however, filing for joint custody.
