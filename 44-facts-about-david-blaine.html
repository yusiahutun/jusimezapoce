David Blaine’s real name is David Blaine White.

David Blaine began practicing magic at a young age.

Blaine’s interest in magic started when he received a deck of cards as a gift at the age of four. Since then, he has dedicated his life to perfecting his craft.

David Blaine gained worldwide fame with his television special “Street Magic.”

In 1997, Blaine shocked viewers with his up-close and personal street magic tricks, which became a sensation and launched his career to new heights.

David Blaine is known for his endurance feats.
