Kelly Osbourne responds to 'weird' rumors over plastic surgery after weight loss

 Posted on September 14, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  506 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Kelly Osbourne has responded to rumors she’s had plastic surgery on her face, which emerged after her impressive 85lb weight loss.

The 38-year-old, former TV star, underwent a gastric sleeve five years ago, losing weight all over her body, including her face. As fans continue to comment on her evolved appearance on social media, she’s addressing the plastic surgery rumors once and for all.

Kelly Osbourne responds to plastic surgery rumors

The Osbournes star called her weight loss surgery ‘the best decision’ she’s ever made, however, has been forced to deny plastic surgery rumors off the back of it.

“It’s weird, because now that I’ve lost weight, everybody is criticizing and trying to figure out what it is that I’ve done, and I really just lost some weight,” Kelly revealed to the Daily Mail.

“It’s just the shape of my face!” she exclaimed.

The new mother expressed that she’d “never had any procedure like plastic surgery”, however, admitted there were things she wanted to get done when she was older, but was “too scared”.

Mom Sharon Osbourne then jumped in to agree as she exclaimed: “Oh, she’s a coward”. Kelly then went on to explain that she’d had Botox, but “that’s it”.

The Fashion Police alum is active on Instagram, posting a range of selfies, as her followers often take to the comments to question her change in appearance. However, she’s now shut down the rumors once and for all.

Although, this isn’t the first time the star has ‘shut down rumors’. While pregnant with her first son, Sidney, she responded online by saying: “Honestly, I’ve only had Botox.”

Aside from facing plastic surgery rumors, Kelly also faced rumors she had a surrogate with her firstborn, as fans claimed they’d never seen a pregnant photo of her.

However, she responded to these by saying she didn’t want to get ‘fat-shamed’ by online trolls.

“There are no pictures of me pregnant because I hid for 9 months because I did not want to get fat-shamed,” she shared.

Sharon Osbourne opens up about her weight loss journey

Just like her daughter, Sharon Osbourne has been through a weight loss journey of her own, using Ozempic.

In the past, she’s spoken about her struggles with the method and the side effects that came with it for her.

“For me, the first few weeks was f***ing s*** because you just throw up all the time, and feel so nauseous,” she told The Club Random with Bill Maher podcast.

She explained that her nausea wore off after a couple of weeks, after which she “felt fine”.

The 70-year-old lost 30 lbs using the drug over a four-month period, and although the method was right for her, speaking to the Daily Mail, Sharon made it clear that she doesn’t support youngsters taking the drug for weight loss, saying it would be “dangerous in the hands of kids”.

“You really have to be an adult to do it to understand what you’re doing to yourself,” she told the publication.
