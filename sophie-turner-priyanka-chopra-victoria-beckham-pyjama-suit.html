Sophie Turner &amp; Priyanka Chopra wear Victoria Beckham

 Posted on September 29, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  322 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Although sisters-in-law Priyanka Chopra and Sophie Turner tend to have very different styles, the A-listers recently had a matching moment in a white, printed pyjama suit from Victoria Beckham's latest collection.

The designer shared images of Turner and Chopra wearing the ensembles on her Instagram account with the hashtag 'JSisters', which is what the duo often label themselves as on social media (in reference to them both having married a Jonas brother).

The two women are both known for their distinctive styles – Chopra tends to channel modern Hollywood glamour for almost any occasion, while Turner is typically a lot more low-key, with plenty of Louis Vuitton making up her red-carpet wardrobe. However, both women are clearly fans of Beckham's button-up style with them each spotted in the look - although the jury is out on whether they both own the two-piece or if one of them was borrowing from her sister-in-law's wardrobe.

Turner dressed hers up for a Roc Nation brunch in Los Angeles in February, tucking it in and teaming with gold sandals and polished waves.

Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

Chopra meanwhile made a low-key appearance in her pyjama suit, wearing it untucked and teaming with a nude handbag, sunglasses, gold chains and gold sandals as she made her way around Paris.

Philippe Blet/Shutterstock//Shutterstock

The chic look is from Victoria Beckham's pre-autumn/winter 2019 collection – and has also been worn by the designer herself, who also teamed it with gold accessories as she attended an event in Qatar.

KARIM JAAFAR//Getty Images

The pyjama suit, which is made up of a white button-up shirt with a light gold dotty print and matching tailored trousers, is perfect for "the plane, the party or the bedroom" Beckham said on her Instagram. It is still available to shop if you want to snap up the celebrity-approved two-piece.
