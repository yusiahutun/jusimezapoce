Megan Fox's 'super hot' plunging mini-dress has fans 'mistaking her for Kim Kardashian'

 Posted on February 19, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  411 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Megan Fox is killing the Instagram game lately, showing off her fiery new red look, and in her latest post, with a plunging white dress, fans are mistaking her for Kim Kardashian.

The Hollywood star recently released her poetry book, Pretty Boys Are Poisonous and has been on many outings promoting since. This week, Megan Fox let her hair down as she stepped out with Machine Gun Kelly at the GQ Men of the Year Party.

Megan Fox fans ‘mistake her’ for Kim Kardashian

Of course, with a fit as fire as this one, it would be rude for Meg not to post it on Instagram. Taking to her 21.6 million Instagram followers, she posted a range of pics in the cut-out white dress, as one was simply not enough.

The figure-hugging white mini dress featured a V-cut neckline with sharp edges, which she paired with clear heels and a clear bag.

The movie star’s signature new red look stood out against the white and was placed into effortless waves.

As she captioned the pic: “a vampire in mugler,” fans rushed to the comments to compliment her. Although, some couldn’t unsee one very famous reality TV star.

“You look like Kim K,” wrote one fan. The comment then received many replies from others, who also agreed.

“They do resemble each other a lot!!!!” exclaimed one.

Another agreed: “Right, I had to double take and realize it was Megan.”

“She looks super hot in everything,” one gushed.

It’s not the first time the two have been mistaken

Earlier this year, Kim modeled her new collection on her SKIMs Instagram page, with fans saying she looked “so different” and compared her to the Jennifer’s Body star.

“@Meganfox is that you?” one user questioned, whilst others said she looked like Selena Gomez.

Megan and MGK are known to be close friends with Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barker, but it looks like she’s also friends with Kim.

On a previous episode of The Kardashians, Kim recalled how Fox reacted when she found out the SKIMs owner was dating Pete Davidson:

“‘Is this s*** for real? Because he asked me for your number months ago,'” the mom of four said in a confessional.

She explained that Megan then told Pete:  “Dude, you have a better shot of me and MGK getting eaten by crocodiles than you [do] ever getting her number. Never gonna happen. Do not ask us.”

Evidently, she was wrong.
