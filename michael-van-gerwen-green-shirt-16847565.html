Michael van Gerwen reveals secret behind always wearing a green shirt

 Posted on April 19, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  186 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The world no.1 is a fearsome competitor at the Oche.

His lethal throwing has bagged him two PDC world titles and a series of other trophies.

And he’s not just consistent when throwing darts - he’s consistent with his wardrobe too.

Van Gerwen sports the same outfit every time he plays - wearing a bright green shirt.

And the Dutchman has revealed why.

Van Gerwen said: “The green shirt is part of me. Everyone knows me in green.

“Sometimes when I’m walking in the street people stop me and say ‘I didn’t recognise you without your darts shirt’.

“It’s not that I picked the green colour just that I used it once and felt comfortable.

“It’s not even my favourite colour! My favourite colour is blue.”

Van Gerwen won 2018 Darts Premier League.

He got revenge against Rob Cross for his defeat in the world championships last year in week one in Dublin where he defeated the Essex player.

And he dominated the entire tournament to underline his credentials as the world's best player.
