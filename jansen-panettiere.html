Career and Professional Life

Talking about his career, In the Disney Channel Original Movie Tiger Cruise, which starred his sister Hayden, Panettiere played a supporting part.

This was one of only two productions that starred both of the siblings; the other was The Forger, in which Jansen provided the voice for Racing Stripes.

The Perfect Game, his subsequent movie, was scheduled to hit theatres on August 8, 2008, but Lionsgate Films chose to delay its release until the spring of 2010.

A squad of young men from Monterrey, Mexico, became the first team from a nation other than the United States to win the Little League World Series, and William Dear’s movie The Perfect Game is based on this true story.

Along with Gavin MacLeod, Panettiere co-stars in Rich Christiano’s movie The Secrets of Jonathan Sperry, which explores friendship and spirituality.

On March 3, 2012, he made an appearance at the Wilshire Ebell Theatre as Elliott Perry alongside Martin Sheen, Christine Lahti, and Jamie Lee Curtis in Dustin Lance Black’s play 8, which recreated the Perry v. Schwarzenegger trial.

The performance was broadcast on YouTube to raise money for the American Foundation for Equal Rights. In 2019, Jansen debuted as a guest role on The Walking Dead on the AMC network and co-starred in How High 2 on MTV.
