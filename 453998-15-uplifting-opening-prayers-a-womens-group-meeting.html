Women are always known to form groups in whichever settings. You will find women groups in learning institutions, government offices, and religious places. Such groups require frequent interactions through meetings and other activities. It is almost always a routine to start the meeting with a prayer during meetings. Opening prayers for a women's group helps in the spiritual growth of any ministry.

Opening prayers for ladies' meetings is a good way of starting any event. Asking for divine intervention as you hold your discussions is an amazing way of preparing everyone to participate in the meeting. There is nothing as beautiful as having active members in a group. Starting a meeting with prayer can influence members' productivity.
