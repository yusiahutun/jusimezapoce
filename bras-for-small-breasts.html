Your Frequently Asked Questions About the Best Bras for Small Breasts

What type of bra is best for a small chest?

Edward Berthelot//Getty Images

The best bra for a small chest depends on a few factors, including how far apart the breasts naturally sit and how much lift or shaping you want from your bra. Shoppers with breasts that are far apart may appreciate a plunge style, while shoppers with closer-set breasts report feeling more comfortable in a scoop or balconette style.

In general, demi-cup or T-shirt bras with shallow cups tend to be popular regardless of specific breast shape or size.
