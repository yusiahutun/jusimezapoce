GALLERY: Kim Kardashian's most memorable hairstyles

 Posted on August 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  254 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

    Kim Kardashian never fails to turn heads with her edgy hairdos, whether she's debuting a platinum blonde look at Paris Fashion Week or opting for a long wavy bob for the Grammys. 

    We take a look back at some of the reality TV queen's most memorable hairstyles... 

    Kim turned heads as she arrived for sister Kylie Jenner's 19th birthday party, with her hair cut into a sleek long bob with an asymmetric length. 

    The reality TV star donned a blonde wavy wig as she starred in Fergie's video alongside stars including Ciara and Chrissy Teigen. 

    Kanye West's wife gave us major hair envy with this eye-catching ombré hairstyle. 

    Supporting Kanye at his New York Fashion Week show, Kim floored fans with this long, poker straight blonde hairstyle - which she later revealed was a wig. 

    The  star joined the bob brigade as she and Kanye attended the Grammys, having had her usually long tresses chopped into a chic shoulder-length long bob. 

    Before she went platinum blonde, Kim had already experimented with honey-hued tones.  

    The mother-of-two caused a social media meltdown when she debuted a new platinum blonde 'do at Paris Fashion Week in 2014. 

    The beauty icon gave us major summer hair inspiration when she added blonde highlights to her brunette hairdo. 

    Although she hasn't rocked a fringe in recent years, Kim used to have a full fringe style which she often wore down for a laidback chic finish. 

    The American star grew out her fringe in 2013, parting it down the middle and wearing her glossy black hair down in cascading waves.
