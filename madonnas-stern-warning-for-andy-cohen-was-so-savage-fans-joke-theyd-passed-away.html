Madonna's 'stern warning' for Andy Cohen was so savage fans joke they'd 'passed away'

 Posted on April 4, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  417 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Madonna has been providing the music and the entertainment on her Celebration Tour. This time, she’s ‘calling out’ Andy Cohen. The Queen of pop was performing at her sold-out show with the Bravo host in the crowd, so of course, she had to have some fun.

It’s not the first special guest the music star has had involved in her New York shows. Last week, she brought her boyfriend, Josh Popper, on stage. The celebrity couple, who share a 35-year age gap, have been linked since February and seemingly went public at her show, as they shared a kiss on stage. Now, it was Andy’s turn to take the spotlight.

Madonna ‘calls out’ Andy Cohen at New York show

The Bravo TV host, was of course sitting front row at the Barclays Center in Brooklyn, New York, and the Vogue star decided to make some comments about his hit show, Watch What Happens Live.

“If you say one more bad thing about me on your show… you’re gonna get in so much trouble,” the singer said before jokingly throwing a bit of her drink.

“You little trouble-making queen!” she continued.

Andy was living for it, as he could be seen mouthing: “I love you” to the Vogue singer.

Watch the interaction here. Warning: contains strong language.

As the video circulates X, formerly known as Twitter, one fan wrote: “I know that was one of the best moments of Andy Cohen’s life.”

“Imagine being eviscerated this badly by Madonna I’d simply pass away,” said another.

Another joked: “He went straight home and wrote a new chapter for his book lol.”

Afterward, the Bravo star took to his Instagram to honor the moment, by writing: “I’m a life-long Madonna fan, so it’s a thrill and an honor to be called a “troublemaking queen” by the Queen of Troublemaking. I BOW!”

The star says it’s a ‘miracle’ she’s alive

The US segment of the tour was postponed due to Madonna’s health scare earlier this year, which she recently addressed on stage.

“The fact that I’m here right now is the f—king miracle,” the Material Girl singer told the crowd, who erupted into cheers.

She also joked that she had to “almost die” to to get all six of her children “in one room” together.

The popstar was rushed to hospital in the summer, following a bacterial infection which left her unresponsive. She was released at the end of June, after spending days in ICU.
