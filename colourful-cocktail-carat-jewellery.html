4 ways colourful jewellery can add oomph to your cocktail attire

 Posted on February 28, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  582 words  |  Larita Shotwell

The festive season is finally upon us, which means it’s time to dust off your cocktail party outfits after all those months in hibernation. From a classic black dress to a contemporary co-ord, we’re excited for our killer looks to finally see the light of day. Add in Carat London’s array of colourful gemstone-style jewellery and it’s never been easier to add some oomph to your attire and create a truly showstopping look.

Here’s a selection of our favourite Christmas party styles (and the jewellery to match), which will work across a multitude of events this celebratory season.

The simple black dress

Getty/Carat

No cocktail party is complete without a smattering of simple black dresses. Understated, elegant and timeless, a clean-cut black dress will see you through years of soirees, making it a truly sustainable option, too. The beauty of this sartorial secret weapon lies in its ability to adapt to any mood and occasion; it’s a blank canvas awaiting your accessories.

Carat London’s elegant Ruby Cluster Earrings, which feature 14ct equivalent of brilliantly cut marquise and ruby pear-cut stones, add a sophisticated dash of rich red to this chic all-black look. And just like a wear-forever black dress, Carat London’s lab-created rubies will see you through endless social events.

The dressed-up jeans

Getty/Carat

If you thought denim was an evening no-no, think again. Anything goes nowadays, it’s just about how you work it. Take your favourite jeans and add a structured statement blazer – preferably one with a little sparkle or shimmer – to elevate them to party-ready status.

Next, add some opulent blue jewels to complement the tone of your denim – Carat London’s spectacular Marina Sapphire Necklace, a true heirloom piece, works perfectly. The artificial sapphires have the same molecular and atomic make-up as the mined kind, but without any of the damaging social or environmental impact.

The classic slip dress

Christian Vierig

Every wardrobe should have a silky slip dress in its arsenal. Find the right one and it will take you from beach to black tie with ease, just like this rosy number. Simply add a statement clutch, barely-there heels and Carat London’s canary yellow jewels. The perfect tone to accompany this pink look, the warm, golden stones add depth, while the diamond white cut stones add a sophisticated, delicate sparkle.

The Oaklee Canary Drop Earrings have just the right amount of detail to add a little interest to the simple slip and ensure it’s dinner-party-ready, while the Alma Canary Radiant Ring with its 3ct equivalent radiant cut stone, is the ultimate cocktail ring – a piece you’ll treasure forever.

The co-ord

Getty/Carat

Meet the cocktail co-ord, perfect for anyone looking for something a little different this party season. Relaxed but perfectly put together, it’s an outfit that combines comfort with style and will have you feeling ready to dazzle your guests. The best part? The top and trousers can be mixed and matched with other pieces from your wardrobe, so you’ll get to wear them even when your social calendar isn’t packed with parties.

Carat London’s Camari Emerald Pear Microset Drops are the perfect partner to this decadent look, with the starburst-shaped design and deep emerald stones emphasising the green star-print pieces.
