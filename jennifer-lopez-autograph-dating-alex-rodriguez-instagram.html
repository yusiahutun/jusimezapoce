Alex Rodriguez Kept Signed Jennifer Lopez Photo from 20 Years Ago &amp; Shared It on Instagram

 Posted on August 13, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  312 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Today in adorable couples, Alex Rodriguez has revealed that he's always been just as big a J.Lo fan as the rest of the world, way before they were even dating.

Related Story

In a ridiculously cute Instagram post, Rodriguez shared a photo of a signed picture of Jennifer Lopez that he acquired almost 20 years ago. That's right—long, long, long before they were ever romantically linked, a young baseball player got a singing and acting superstar to sign a photo for him. And he never threw it out.

Realizing just how crazy this coincidence is, Rodriguez captioned the autograph, "#TBT to almost 20 years ago when a big time global pop superstar signed a pic for a young ball player #lifecanbeafunnythingsometimes #pictureperfect #keepsake #jlo." Clearly, Rodriguez is pretty happy about the way things turned out:

Lopez and Rodriguez crossed paths several times, before eventually starting to date in 2017.

In fact, back when Lopez was married to fellow singer Marc Anthony, she was pictured alongside A-Rod on the baseball field in 2005:

Mike Ehrmann//Getty Images

In March 2018, Lopez revealed to Harper's BAZAAR that she wasn't rushing into marriage again (she's been divorced three times). The Wedding Planner star explained, "I do believe in marriage, and I would love to grow old with somebody in a committed relationship. But I'm not forcing anything right now. It's good, it's healthy; we communicate well. We understand each other's lives in a way that most other people couldn't."

Whether or not J.Lo and A-Rod ever decide to tie the knot, it's clear that they're extremely happy together right now.
