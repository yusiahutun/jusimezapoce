Like every other woman who has gotten engaged before her, Behati Prinsloo cannot seem to stop admiring her new engagement bling.

The Victoria's Secret stunner was spotted excitedly examining her giant diamond ring after boyfriend and Maroon 5 frontman Adam Levine proposed to her.

The 24-year-old was seen outside the Mercer Kitchen restaurant in New York on Wednesday night, where she chatted with friends happily while smoking a cigarette and flaunting her ring for all to see.

Engagement bling: Behati Prinsloo flashed her large diamond engagement ring in New York on Wednesday night after getting engaged to Maroon 5 frontman Adam Levine

The model, who got engaged to Levine over the weekend, was seen for the first time since the big news, and she looked overjoyed.

Nice work Adam: The ring appears to be rather large

Wearing a pair of tiny denim Daisy Dukes and a Motorhead white tank top that bared her midriff, it is easy to see why Adam is so smitten with the Namibian beauty.

Make-up free, Behati wore a black wide-brimmed hat on her head and opted for casual slip-on shoes for the outing.

On Thursday morning, Behati continued to gush about her ring, telling ET Canada that it was 'perfect!'

She also revealed that Adam got down on bended knee during his proposal.

'I am so happy and excited and still can't believe it's true!' she said. 'It was a one-knee thing! It was serious, it was very old school!'

The couple have yet to start planning their big day and are currently 'just celebrating with our friends and family at the moment.'

The supermodel is basking in engagement bliss since the The Voice judge got down on bended knee over the weekend.

The 34-year-old singer proposed to her in Los Angeles after the couple recently reunited.

'Adam Levine and his girlfriend Behati Prinsloo are excited to announce they are engaged to be married,' Adam's rep told People magazine earlier this week.

'The couple recently reunited and Adam proposed this weekend in Los Angeles.'

News of the engagement may come as a surprise to some, as Adam was recently seen on holiday with Sports Illustrated beauty Nina Agdal after he and Prinsloo split shortly after the New Year.

He had been reportedly 'having fun' with the 21-year-old stunner since early spring.

'It's still new,' a source close to the singer told Us Weekly when the romance was first reported. 'Behati was in and out, but they finally ended it. It wasn't an abrupt ending.'
