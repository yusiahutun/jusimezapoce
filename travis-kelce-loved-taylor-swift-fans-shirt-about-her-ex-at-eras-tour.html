Travis Kelce loved Taylor Swift fan's shirt about her ex at Eras Tour

 Posted on July 30, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  665 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Trav knows All Too Well about his girlfriend’s back catolog. A happy Swiftie, who attended her Eras Tour before they even got together, must have heard the ins and outs about her ex-boyfriends by listening to her albums. But Travis Kelce’s feelings over Taylor’s exes is crystal clear when a fan showed him his Eras Tour t-shirt last week.

Celebrity couples have a lot more baggage than usual relationships, in the sense if they date other stars, their love story is all over the front pages. They can’t hide from it. So when a celebrity connects with someone new, it must be difficult to push aside the headlines. For Taylor Swift, she’s been singing about the highs and lows of her dating life for her entire career. Therefore, any new person on the scene will be pretty clued up over the long list of her ex-lovers if they feel a spark. When the tight end walked into her life, he was already a huge fan, so probably knew a lot of the lore behind her back catalog. Travis Kelce couldn’t help but smile and laugh at a fan’s shirt shading her ex at the Eras Tour last week, so it’s clear how he feels about them. Awkward.

Travis Kelce laughs at fan’s shirt at the Eras Tour dissing Taylor’s ex

If the 34-year-old was a fan of Brokeback Mountain, he might not be anymore.

One Tay-Tay fan posted a hilarious clip on TikTok, showing how their mission was for Travis Kelce to see his Eras Tour shirt while attending her concert in Australia.

The front of tee says: “Taylor loves Travis Kelce so I love Travis Kelce.”

But he turns around and it has a photo of a movie star crossed out, with the words: “F You Jake Gyllenhaal”.

Yikes. Not team Jake then?

We then see the guy finding Travis Kelce in the VIP section at the Eras Tour, behind the security gate, and shows him the front of his shirt.

Trav, brother of center Jason Kelce, smiles – I mean the front is very lovely – and applauds the creation. Then the fan turns around so he can see the back, which the NFL star leans over to make sure he can read it all. When he finds out it’s dissing the film star he starts laughing.

Probably wasn’t expecting that.

‘Pick a toxic ex-boyfriend’

Not all fans felt the shirt had the right ‘ex-boyfriend’ on the back to grab Travis Kelce’s attention at the Eras Tour.

One said: “At least pick a toxic ex like John Mayer. It just didn’t work out with Jake”.

But a Swiftie wrote back: “Watch All Too Well short movie, you will understand”.

Another claimed: “That’s the first genuine smile I’ve seen Trav do in Australia”.

And a third added: “He definitely told Taylor about this shirt later on”.

While a fourth explained they saw the guy in his shirt at the gig itself. She said: “I saw him & complimented the shirt and he goes, ‘read the back when I walk away’, what a slay!”

Travis exchanges friendship bracelets with Swifties

Although the Kansas City Chiefs player is back in the US – he’s already been seen partying in Las Vegas – he had a great time Down Under.

However, one body language expert did pick up on him being ‘uncomfortable’ on a date with Taylor at Sydney Zoo.

Pushing that aside, he was seen exchanging friendship bracelets with one Swiftie in a cute video. This is something sacred to Tay Tay fans, who make extra bracelets ahead of the concert to swap with other devoted listeners.

In the video, titled ‘Just two new friends exchanging bracelets’, we see the fan grab the attention of the 34-year-old in the VIP booth. He holds his hand out waiting for her to threw it and manages to catch it in one go.

He then reads it and salutes the girl in the crowd.

So cute.
