Amit Sadh (born 5 June 1983) is an Indian actor. He is known for playing supporting roles in films such as Kai Po Che (2013), the comedy Guddu Rangeela (2015), and the political thriller Sarkar 3 (2017). He began his acting career in Star Plus' teen drama Kyun Hota Hai Pyarrr, and later appeared in television reality show Bigg Boss (Hindi Season 1). He also played the role of Kshitij, a lawyer, in Shobna Desai's soap opera Durgesh Nandinii that aired on Sony Entertainment Television. Sadh made his film debut with the 2010 horror film, Phoonk 2. He appeared in Amazon Prime's web-series Breathe (2018) and its second season Breathe: Into the Shadows (2020) playing the role of Inspector Kabir Sawant. In the new series Avrodh the Siege Within on Sony Liv, he plays Major Videep Singh.
