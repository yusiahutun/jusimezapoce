Haley Reinhart is a singer who first rose to fame after placing 3rd in the 10th season of American Idol. She has performed songs for movies and T.V. shows. Undone for Step Up Revolution (2012) and Free on 90210 (2012) are the notable ones among them. She also gave her voice to Bill Murphy on F Is for Family (2015-2018).

Born to musician parents, she started singing at the age of eight. Haly has been a part of multiple jazz ensembles. She is also widely recognized for her collaboration with Postmodern Jukebox (a.k.a. PMJ), a rotational musical collective.

American Idol Run

Haley first auditioned for American Idol season 9 in 2010. Then, she did not advance to the Hollywood round. She tried once again for the 10th season in 2011, and finally got into the top 3.

But, her accomplishment with her stellar performance didn’t satisfy her fans, also known as Haliens. They reportedly got angered with the criticisms Haley faced from Randy Jackson and Jennifer Lopez.

Celebrities such as Chris Cooley, Tom Hanks, and Ellen DeGeneres were also among Haley’s supportive fans.

Boyfriend

The American Idol alum is dating a musician named Drew Dolan. The two have been together since June of 2015.

Haley Reinhart with boyfriend Drew Dolan in January 2020 (Photo: Drew Dolan's Instagram)

Drew was born and raised in Chicago. His birthday is on 14 November.

Past Relationships

Haley and American Idol co-contestant Casey Abrams sparked romance rumors during their run on the show. He clarified his relation with Haley in a conference call after he got voted off the show. He referred to her as a very special musical friend and further added that they were not dating.

Haley dated R&B singer, Stefano Langone in the past. They were first linked romantically in August of 2011. Their relationship, however, was only confirmed in January of 2012 by James Durbin, another American Idol star.

Haley Reinhart's Age & Family

Haley Elizabeth Reinhart was born on 9 September 1990 in Wheeling, Illinois. Her parents are Patti Miller-Reinhart and Harry Reinhart, both of whom were part of a band called Midnight. Her mother was the singer and the bandleader.

Haley also has a younger sister named Angela Reinhart.

Education

She attended Mark Twain Elementary School, O.W. Holmes Middle School, and Wheeling High School in Wheeling. She moved to Palatine after graduating high school in 2009 and enrolled in Harper College, where she studied jazz.

Net Worth

According to celebritynetworth.com, Haley has a net worth of $1.5 million.

Interesting Facts

  • Haley has a height of 5 feet 2 inches (1.57 meters).

  • The American Idol alum performed at the 2009 Montreux Jazz Festival in Switzerland with her high school's jazz band.
