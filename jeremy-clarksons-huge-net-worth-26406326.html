Jeremy Clarkson's girlfriend is Lisa Hogan, a former Irish model and actress.

How much is Jeremy Clarkson's net worth?

Jeremy Clarkson's net worth is estimated to be around £45 million, according to Wealthy Gorilla.

Starting out as a journalist on local papers, Clarkson eventually began writing about cars and helped set up the Motoring Press Agency in 1984.

He first appeared on Top Gear in 1988 and became increasingly well-known, featuring on the programme more frequently.

In 2002, he began presenting Top Gear in its new format, the success of which meant Clarkson raced to fame quicker than reasonably priced Chevrolet Lacetti around a race track near Guilford.

After leaving Top Gear in disgrace in 2015, Clarkson, Hammond and May embarked upon their new project - The Grand Tour - in a multi-million Amazon Prime deal.

How much does Jeremy Clarkson earn from the Grand Tour?

The exact amount Jeremy Clarkson is paid for his services on The Grand Tour is unknown, but believed to be a share of £160million.

This was the figure quoted - though described by Clarkson as 'nonsense' - that the famous trio were given by Amazon for the new series. A figure that included production costs.

Clarkson said of his high pay packet: "How could you be paid £100,000 for an hour’s work?” Well, it isn’t an hour’s work because every single time you put your foot out the door, within a minute someone’s going to ask you a selfie."
