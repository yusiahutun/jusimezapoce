Who is Kati Morton:

Kati Morton is a famous YouTube Star. She was born on October 13, 1983 and her birthplace is Washington. Kati is also well known as, YouTuber known for her self-titled channel where she posts advice and educational videos about mental health issues, from suicide to eating disorders. She also developed her own mental health social network. 

Kati is originated from United States. In October 2015, she created the video “Thought Dropping” with My Drunk Kitchen’s Hannah Hart for World Mental Health Day.
