9 Best Bridal Mary Jane Flats, Inspired by Margaret Qualley

 Posted on April 22, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  809 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

The All-Around Elegant Flats

Le Monde Beryl Silk-Satin Mary Jane Flats

The All-Around Elegant Flats

Le Monde Beryl Silk-Satin Mary Jane Flats

Credit: MatchesFashion

Mary Janes are Le Monde Beryl’s most well-known silhouette, so the label is a must on any list of bridal flats. This pair has the same sheen as Qualley’s wedding-day flats, with a low heel and lightly padded insole.

Sizes: 5½–11½

Colors: White satin

The Insider-Favorite Flat

Sandy Liang Exclusive White Pointe Mary-Jane Ballerina Flats

The Insider-Favorite Flat

Sandy Liang Exclusive White Pointe Mary-Jane Ballerina Flats

Credit: SSENSE

Sandy Liang’s cult-beloved ballet flats were just released in white satin exclusively for Ssense’s bridal edit. I suspect the most runway-inclined brides will cherish the pointe-shoe-inspired square toe and dainty buckle as much as their wedding gown—these flats are that rarely in stock.

Sizes: 35–41 IT

Colors: White

Materials: Satin, leather, rubber

The Wedding-and-Beyond Mary Jane

Tamara Mellon Minty Mary Jane

The Wedding-and-Beyond Mary Jane

Tamara Mellon Minty Mary Jane

Credit: Tamara Mellon

Tamara Mellon released her long-awaited take on the Mary Jane this year, complete with an elongated toe box and a teeny-tiny heel. The cream patent fits a bridal dress code, but also has everyday-outfit appeal, thanks to its more cream-adjacent shade and button-embellished strap.

Sizes: 5–12

Colors: White patent, red patent, black patent

The Sweet Satin Mary Jane

Loeffler Randall Leonie Cream Ballet Flat

The Sweet Satin Mary Jane

Loeffler Randall Leonie Cream Ballet Flat

I got married in Loeffler Randall’s famous Camellia heels, but I wish I’d had the foresight to also order these little satin flats. Having now tried on pairs in the store, I can attest that they have a soft sole, an elastic strap that’s secure without pinching, and a dainty, rounded toe that will look gorgeous with midi and maxi dresses. My bridal buyer’s remorse is what pushed me to order a chocolate-brown pair for fall; future brides, consider a pair for your nuptials and daily wear.

Sizes: 5–12

Colors: White, black, chocolate, terracotta, and more

The Diamond-Studded Flat

Jimmy Choo Bing Flat

The Diamond-Studded Flat

Jimmy Choo Bing Flat

Credit: Jimmy Choo

If there’s ever a day to make your accessories sparkle, it’s your wedding day. Jimmy Choo’s Mary Jane flat comes with a bejeweled strap for head-to-toe shimmer, plus a comfortable slide-on silhouette.

Sizes: 34–43 EU

Colors: Linen patent, black patent

The Mary Janes With a Heel

Repetto Rose Mary Janes

The Mary Janes With a Heel

Repetto Rose Mary Janes

Credit: Repetto

A three-centimeter block heel still qualifies as a flat in my book. This patent leather pair provides a little lift for standing tall at the altar, with a gentle pitch that won’t be pinching your toes by the end of the big day.

Sizes: 35–42 FR

Colors: White, black, red, pink, and more

The High-Contrast Flats

Co Leather Ballerina Flats

The High-Contrast Flats

Co Leather Ballerina Flats

The perfect pair of Mary Janes for a minimalist bride does exist, and it hails from Los Angeles–based label Co. A streamlined white leather upper and stark black elasticated strap can go from the rehearsal dinner to the morning-after brunch.

Sizes: 36–41 IT

Colors: White/black strap

The Studded Patent Flats

Valentino Garavani Tiptoe Patent Leather Ballerinas

The Studded Patent Flats

Valentino Garavani Tiptoe Patent Leather Ballerinas

Now 60% Off

Credit: Farfetch

For the Valentino Rockstud collection devotee: a pair of wedding-day flats embellished with the designer’s signature decal. (After all, the best bridal look should ultimately also reflect your style away from the aisle.) The angular pointed toe provides the hint of juxtaposition suited to a tea-length dress with a confectionary full skirt.

Sizes: 35½–42 IT

Colors: White

The Twinkle-Toe Mary Janes

Chanel Cloth Ballet Flats

The Twinkle-Toe Mary Janes

Chanel Cloth Ballet Flats

Credit: Vestiaire Collective

If you’re trying to channel Qualley’s outfit through a designer lens, there are archival pairs fitting a bridal moment, if you know where to look. This shimmering pair with Chanel’s signature cap toe is perfect for dancing into the morning under the glow of an after-party disco ball. (Pairs in additional sizes can be found on Vestiaire Collective, the RealReal, and Fashionphile.)

Sizes: 36½

Colors: Silver glitter/black
