Jessica Mediate who admitted being a professional golf watcher is popularly known as the current and the second wife of the golfing champ, Rocco Mediate.

How It Began For Jessica Mediate & Husband Rocco

Jessica Mediate whose life drastically changed after her marital relation with her husband, Rocco Mediate, interestingly knew her then-future five years prior to their wedding which took place in January 2014 in Hawaii.

Jessica Mediate and her husband Rocco Mediate on their wedding. SOURCE: Rocco Mediate

Of course, she knew about the famous golfer but didn't formally meet Rocco personally till both of their mutual friends introduced them. More precisely, their love story didn't start until three years later of the first time they met.

Fast forward in 2013, Mediate was surprised when her longterm boyfriend got on his knees with a serious face and asked her to marry him. To add, it was the same year the PGA Tour winner finalized his divorce with his first wife of twenty years, Linda Newell with whom she split in 2008.

Jessica Mediate and her husband, Rocco Mediate. SOURCE: Pinterest

Following the next year on 20th January 2014, Rocco said 'I Do' for the second time with his then-new bride, Jessica who also accepted to be a stepmother to her new husband's three sons from his previous marriage. Interestingly, their wedding took place right before the golf pro of more than three decades had to face competition.

The couple then welcomed their daughter, Francesca Rose Mediate in May 2015. Francesca often makes her appearance in her daddy's game to show her support along with her mother.

Also on the bright side, Jessica and Rocco's marriage has been sheer blissful as the two have been together through the ups and downs life threw at them. Not to mention, Jessica has been respectfully and lovingly taking on her role as a wife, from never missing his husband's golf games and being there through Rocco's alcohol addiction which he got rid of only in 2017.
