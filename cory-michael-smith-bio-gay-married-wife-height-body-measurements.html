Cory Michael Smith Bio.

To get to know him better, Cory Michael Smith was born on November 14, 1986, in Columbus, Ohio, United States. There the actor and his older brother, Chad, were raised by his parents.

For his education, Smith attended Hillard Darby High School and graduated there. From there he went to Otterbein University, where he studied art-music theatre and jazz piano.

Since acting seems so natural to him, it is very hard to believe that acting was not his first dream. He first wanted to become a lawyer and then a pianist before he decided to become an actor.

Smith is not one of the oldest actors there is. The 30-year-old did not start as an actor until 2014 when he made his debut at Camp X-Ray. That same year, he appeared in another short film, Dog Food. In 2015 he starred in another film, Carol, and in 2017 his next film was Wonders Truck.
