50+ best questions to ask celebrities on Instagram and Facebook

 Posted on September 7, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1162 words  |  Martina Birk

Social media has improved interactions between celebrities and their fans. Platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter give fans direct access to their favourite celebrities. However, sometimes celebrities want a deeper connection with their fans, so they put an "Ask Me Anything" segment on their pages. Fans respond by typing questions to ask celebrities as their idol replies. This feature is ideal if you want to know more about your idols and what they are about.

When given a chance to ask your favourite star something, don’t go for the obvious. Celebrities get all manner of questions and attention every day, be different. Refrain from repeating questions they have answered before.

Mix funny questions with witty, serious, and even personal questions on Instagram. Some celebrities do not mind telling everything. Some people enjoy being an open book, and if your favourite actor or singer has this kind of personality, don’t waste the chance to know them deeper.

Personal questions to ask celebrities on Instagram

Many celebrities want to cover their personal lives from the world. This is because fans and critics can overanalyze their lifestyles, affecting their families. Some celebrities, however, will love it when you ask about their personal lives.

Having followed a celebrity actor for a long time, you are bound to know how they respond to questions. If your favourite chef loves sharing int*imate moments of their life, shoot your questions nonstop.

  • How many scandals has your team covered up for you?
  • Is Book smart or street smart?
  • Do you always say hi to other celebrities when you see them, even if you do not know them personally?
  • Which social media platform do you hate the most?
  • How many hours a day do you work?
  • How often do you Google yourself?
  • Does fame bring you more joy than when you were not known?
  • How often do you work out at the gym?
  • What is one low-budget meal you enjoy?
  • What is your dating deal breaker?
  • Have you ever worn an outfit you hated just for marketing purposes?
  • Do you pay family to support you perpetually?
  • Do you conduct interviews for your personal assistants and housekeepers, or does your team do that for you?
  • When was the last time your heart was broken?
  • Do you know someone who has ever leaked your story, yet you play cool with them?
  • Have you ever disguised yourself just to go to a public place where people would judge you?
  • Were you taught how to walk in heels or pose on a red carpet?
  • How many facials have you had?
  • How many times have you been told to change something about yourself?
  • Have you ever been rejected after shooting your shot? How did you handle it?
