Chloe Stroll Dad

Chloe Stroll was born to her dad Lawrence Stroll and Claire-Anne Stroll. Lawrence is best known as a Canadian billionaire businessman and part-owner of the Ashton Martin F1 Team. He was also masterminded behind Michael Kors’ hugely successful IPO in 2011 with business partner Silas Chou, a Hong Kong fashion tycoon. He is reportedly worth $3 billion.

The bulk of Stroll’s fortune comes from selling his shares in the American fashion brand; he sold the last of his stake in 2014.

In August 2018, Chloe’s father led a group of investors to buy Formula One racing team Force India for £90 million plus the assumption of £15 million in debt. He also served as executive chairman after leading a $235.6 million (£182 million) investment in car company Aston Martin in early 2020.

Moreover, Lawrence is also a collector of a vintage Ferrari. Back in 2013, he purchased one of the cars for $27.5 million.

Talking about his real estate investment, in 2014, he bought a full-floor unit in One57, known as “the billionaire building,” in Manhattan for $55.5 million. He sold it for $53.9 million in 2018. Moreover, he sold his trophy penthouse which was previously owned by Oprah Winfrey for $20 million.

He had bought an 11-story condo building known as Villa Del Mare for $13.5 million in February 2014 through his company, property records show.

Fun Fact: In the document of Force India, Lawrence wrote his real last name “Strulovitch.”
