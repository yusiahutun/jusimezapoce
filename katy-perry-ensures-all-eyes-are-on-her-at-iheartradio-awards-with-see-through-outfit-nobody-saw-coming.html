Katy wasn’t shying away from the cameras at the award show, as she posed in a mesh dress that ‘proudly’ showed off her black underwear. It was a bold and daring look for the star, but she’s not hitting Bianca Censori‘s level just yet…

Katy Perry stuns at iHeartRadio Music Awards in see-through dress

The Firework singer took the opportunity to show off her toned figure and abs at Monday night’s show, in a see-through mesh dress.

Opting for all black, the music star paired the look with some high-heeled black boots, accessorizing with a chic pair of sunglasses, also matching the color theme.

Her black hair was styled into light waves, but she did add a pop of color as she tied red ribbons on the dress to complete the look.

The star ‘hangs out’ with Beyonce and fans can’t cope
