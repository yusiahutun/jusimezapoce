Sydney Sweeney's White Bra Served as the Main Character of Her Latest Red Carpet Look

 Posted on August 6, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  269 words  |  Martina Birk

From the no-pants trend to cutouts, visible bras, and sheerness overload, 2023 was naked dressing's best year yet — and Sydney Sweeney is closing out its final days with the ultimate trifecta. 

On Monday morning, the actress attended a screening of her new film Anyone But You in Sydney, Australia, and for the occasion, she combined three naked trends into one dress on the red carpet. Wearing a sheer white gown with an open neckline that plunged down to her waist, Sweeney's matching bra was fully exposed in front, as well in the back — thanks to the dress's double deep V design.

The gown's see-through fabric also revealed a pair of coordinating high-waisted briefs underneath while elegantly gathering at her hips. Making her bra really stand out all on its own, she accessorized minimally, adding only a pair of cream-colored platform pumps, a set of glistening cocktail rings, and diamond earrings to her look.

Sydney wore her bust-length blonde hair down in soft bombshell waves with a middle part, and finished off her glam with pink lipgloss, black liner, and shimmery eyeshadow. 

This wasn't Sydney's only wow-worthy style moment on the press tour. Earlier this month, the Euphoria star was spotted in New York City promoting her upcoming romantic comedy (also starring Glen Powell) wearing a sparkly take on the power suit with wide-leg trousers covered in sequins and a matching halter-neck vest with nothing underneath. Meanwhile, at the New York premiere of the film, she opted for another naked look that included a sheer and shimmery custom Miu Miu gown dripping in rhinestones.
