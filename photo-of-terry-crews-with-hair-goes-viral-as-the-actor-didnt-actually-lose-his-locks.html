Terry Crews with hair might shock you as the actor didn't actually lose his locks

 Posted on September 20, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  440 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Throwback photos of actor Terry Crews with hair might shock you as the star didn’t actually lose his locks and he is not bald at all.

That’s right! It turns out that a lot of work goes into maintaining Terry Crews’ signature bald look. He admitted in an interview he shaves his head every day, making him bald by choice. Now a throwback photo of the star in his youth with a full head of hair has the celebrity‘s fans in awe.

Throwback photos of Terry Crews with hair might shock you

Terry Crews is an American actor, television host, and former football player who is widely known for his iconic bald appearance as he has rocked no hair since becoming famous.

A TikTok video shared by @the_beautyofcinema shows ‘photos of bald actors before losing their hair.’

The clip transitions through images of iconic actors, starting with Terry Crews.

Over on Instagram, Terry Crews has previously shared a throwback photo taken back when he was only 18 years old.

In the black and white photograph, Crews is rocking a full head of hair and his classic shining smile.

Born on 30 July 1968 in Flint, Michigan, the celebrity is currently age 55 years old – suggesting the photo was taken in 1987.

Terry is actually bald by choice

In a 2018 TV appearance on Jimmy Kimmel Live, Terry Crews talks about being bald, as his latest movie at the time, ‘Sorry to Bother You’ shows him with a full head of hair.

Crews was asked by the talk show host if he could actually grow hair.

The answer sure did shock many, as Terry Crews responded: “I am living a lie, because I am really not bald at all. I shave my head every day.”

Crews revealed: “See, when it grows in, people are like, ‘Who is he? What is that?’ And people get really disturbed. So I’ve been this way for almost 30 years.”

Yes, the White Chicks actor can grow a full head of hair – a style that he says makes him look completely different.

Things got a little hairy on Terry Crews’ recent Jimmy Kimmel Live appearance in January 2024.

Kimmel presents his guest with a box of wigs for Crews to try on, varying from The Beatles style, Star Wars-themed, country hair-do, and a classic 90’s hairstyle.

It is no secret that Crews likes to rock different hairstyles every so often, whether it be his own hair or artificial wigs.

In a 2022 episode of The Jennifer Hudson Show, Terry dressed as fellow actor Mr. T for his Halloween costume.
