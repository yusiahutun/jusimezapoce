Latin Rock Icons Man Confirm U.S. Dates For Rayando El Sol Tour

 Posted on August 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  344 words  |  Martina Birk

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) – Veteran Latin rock act Maná has today (Feb. 22) revealed a return to the road with the announcement of their forthcoming Rayando El Sol Tour.

The tour’s name is a nod to Maná’s critically acclaimed single also titled “Rayando El Sol,” which was one of the band’s first great successes and effectively kicked off a remarkable career that has included 4 Grammy Awards, 8 Latin Grammy Awards, and 19 Billboard Latin Music Awards.

Produced by Live Nation, the 20+ date tour is scheduled to begin September 4th in Corpus Christi, TX and will make stops in Houston, San Diego, Brooklyn and more, before wrapping November 27th in Sacramento, CA. The U.S. outing will also include 4 nights at The Forum in Los Angeles, CA between September and November, making it the largest run of dates at The Forum for any Latin act in history.

Tickets go on sale to the general public beginning Friday, March 1st at 11am local time at LiveNation.com.
