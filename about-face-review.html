10 TikTok-Viral Products I'd Buy Again

With so many popular products, an About-face review is long overdue. About-Face has quickly established itself as one of the most innovative makeup brands on the market. From its Cheek Freak Blush Balm to its newly launched foundation The Performer, About-Face knows a thing or two about creating TikTok-viral products. Although I haven’t tried any of the brand’s products before this, I knew so much about them from my TikTok For You page alone. After the brand launched at Ulta, I was constantly swatching everything in-store and seeing people rave about the products online. So naturally, it felt necessary to actually test everything out for myself and see if the products live up to the hype. 

Created by Halsey, all of About-Face’s products are vegan, clean, and cruelty-free. About-Face strives to create makeup without rules in order to promote self-expression. One of the things that struck me most was how colorful all of the products are. With so many shades, the brand is practically begging you to get more creative with your looks. 

All of About-Face’s products are simple yet elevated. I hate heavy foundation, but I thought the Performer was a really easy to wear foundation that didn’t feel heavy on my skin. Its Smokesticks, Shadowsticks, and Matte Fluid Eye Paints were all right up my alley and made applying eyeshadow quick and easy. I usually don’t wear eyeshadow on a daily basis but these products made it easy to. I love how the brand emphasizes that fun makeup doesn’t have to take hours to do—all of the products are extremely user friendly and that’s one thing I always look for.

Have you been dying to try some of About-Face’s most TikTok-viral products? These are the ones I’ve tried that I know I’ll be using until they run out. 

  • The Performer

    about-face The Performer

    I am not a foundation girly by any means, so I’m always skeptical of trying out new ones. But after seeing so much TikTok buzz about this one, I had to try it out. The Performer pleasantly surprised me. I love how lightweight it feels and its skin-focused formula. My shade M1NEUTRAL looked on the warm side when I first applied it, but it blended in really nicely and didn’t look orangey afterwards. Although TikTokers have expressed their dislike for the applicator and its potential to be unsanitary, I actually love it and found it made application very easy.

  • Matte Fluid Eye Paint

    about-face Matte Fluid Eye Paint

    By far one of about-face’s most iconic products, the Matte Fluid Eye Paint was one I was dying to try. This liquid eyeshadow comes in so many bright shades and is super pigmented. In addition to using it as traditional eyeshadow, I also love using it as a liquid eyeliner. (It’s great as eyeliner—just remember to work quickly as it dries down fast.)

  • Cheek Freak Blush Balm

    about-face Cheek Freak Blush Balm

    The Cheek Freak Blush Balm has been constantly popping up on my TikTok For You page—mostly because of its unique balm formula that melts into a liquid. If you’re looking for an easy-to-apply blush, this is a great choice. It’s very pigmented, so a little goes a long way!

  • Fractal Glitter Eye Paint

  • Smokestick

    about-face Smokestick

    If you’re a fan of the indie sleaze smudged eyeliner look, you need the Smokestick! It’s dual-ended with a crayon and a shadow-loaded sponge to get the perfect smoky look. I used the crayon first to make a wing and then the sponge to smoke it out. It’s super easy to use and perfect for nights out.

  • Shadowstick

    about-face Shadowstick

    about-face loves easy-to-use eyeshadow, and the Shadowstick is no exception. Like their other eye products, the Shadowstick comes in so many amazing shades. (As well as matte or pearly finish.) If you’re not sure about liquid eyeshadow, this is a great option, although it’s not quite as pigmented as the Matte Fluid Eye Paints.

  • Shadow Fix Eye Primer

    about-face Shadow Fix Eye Primer

    For all of your eyeshadow looks, you need the Shadow Fix Eye Primer. Eyeshadow primer is vital in making your eyeshadow last all day, and this one is great for locking down product and preventing creases.

  • Light Lock Lip Gloss

    about-face Light Lock Lip Gloss

    I’ve been really into lip gloss lately, and this Light Lock Lip Gloss is a much needed addition to my collection. It feels great on the lips and gives the prettiest shine. Not to mention, like all about-face products, the packaging is just stunning.

  • Lip Fix Lip Primer

    about-face Lip Fix Lip Primer

    I had no idea lip primer was a thing, but now the Lip Fix Lip Primer is a staple in my makeup routine! If you struggle with dry or cracked lips, this primer is perfect for prepping your lips for product.

  • Vinyl Effect Eye Gloss

    about-face Vinyl Effect Eye Gloss

    If you love editorial makeup, the Vinyl Effect Eye Gloss is a must. Glossy lids are the perfect way to add dewiness to your lids and this gloss does that perfectly. Although it’s not an everyday look, I love using this when I want to try something different.

  • Paint-It Matte Lip Color

    about-face Paint-It Matte Lip Color

    On Sale 50% off

    I’ll be honest, matte lip color just isn’t for me. I tried really hard to like this Paint-It Matte Lip Color, but I would much rather wear a more glossy and hydrating lip product. If you like a matte lip, this one is really pigmented so it might be worth the try.

  • Cherry Pick Lip Color Butter

    about-face Cherry Pick Lip Color Butter

    Since I love hydrating lip products, I was expecting to love this Cherry Pick Lip Color Butter. But sadly, it missed the mark for me. The product itself is great but the packaging was hard to use. I prefer a doe foot or a traditional lipstick applicator, this one is smaller and so it’s harder to apply. Not to mention, you have to be careful not to twist up too much because it doesn’t twist back down.
