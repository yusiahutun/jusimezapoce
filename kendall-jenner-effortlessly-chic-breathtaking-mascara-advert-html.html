Kendall Jenner looks effortlessly chic in slinky black dress for 'breathtaking' new mascaraadvert

 Posted on February 1, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  576 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Kendall Jenner is the star of a new commercial for L'Oreal Paris Panorama Mascara.

The 28-year-old model — who recently spent time with pal Hailey Bieber — wore a clinging black dress with an asymmetric choker neckline for the gig.

Her glossy brunette hair was parted down the middle and pulled off her face to effectively show off the product.

Jenner wore large, bulbous, statement gold earrings that grazed her defined jawline.

The reality television personality shared the content on Instagram on Tuesday, writing in the caption, 'my secret to breathtaking lashes @lorealparis #PanoramaMascara.'

In addition to the eye-catching mascara, Kendall captivated with blushed cheeks and a mauve, matte lipstick.

The post has so far collected more than 1.1 million likes from her 294 million followers on the popular social media app.

The official L'Oreal Instagram account began teasing the new advert earlier this week with brief excerpts from the shoot.

A caption shared days ago read, 'Get ready to embark on a love story. Something big is coming… 1.4x bigger, to be precise. #StayTuned for the big reveal.'

And earlier this week Kendall appeared in a mascara application tutorial with makeup artist Mary Phillips.

The caption said: 'A single swipe transforms your lashes into a flawless, voluminous masterpiece. Elevate your lashes at the link in bio.'

The Hulu star was wrapped in a plush white robe as she used a small mirror to show fans how to use the new eyelash tool.

The latest Instagram post from the brand has a caption that says, 'Even @kendalljenner can't get enough! Are you prepared to fall in love with the before and after magic, revealing eyes up to 1.4x bigger.'
