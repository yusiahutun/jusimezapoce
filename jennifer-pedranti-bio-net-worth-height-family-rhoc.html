Jennifer Pedranti Family

Jennifer Pedranti comes from a family of five.

Her father, Bob Ellsworth is the founder of Ellsworth Construction. He started the company back in the 70s working on a [sealcoat machine] performing crack sealing and striping.

As for her mother, Kristin D Ellsworth is a Stroud High School graduate who turned 68 in December 2022.

“All the good I’ve done and all the mistakes I’ve made… She loves me the same,” Jennifer dedicated an IG post to her on Mother’s Day 2022.

Now, talking about her siblings, Jennifer’s 2-year-younger brother Nathan Ellsworth followed in his father’s footsteps and worked as a Manager at Ellsworth Construction. He went to the University of Tulsa to study finance to be qualified for this job.

It seems Nathan has been a part of the Ellsworth team since 2004. He also holds a position on the Board of Directors of the Oklahoma-Building Chapter of the Association of General Contractors.

In 2023, Nathan lived in Tulsa, Oklahoma with his wife Kendall Costigan Ellsworth, the owner of Rivere Real Estate Group.

Likewise, Jennifer’s 5-year-older brother, Andrea N Jason Ellsworth also worked in Ellsworth Construction as its COO. With his advanced understanding of business administration across various sectors, Jason played a vital role in developing the family business.

Sadly, Jennifer’s maternal grandmother, Lois C. Shepard took her last breath on  January 23, 2008, at the age of 79. She was a member of the First United Methodist Church and worked for Lincoln Bank as a Teller for many years.

Also, Jennifer’s paternal grandmother, Elsie Virginia Baker Ellsworth passed away on, November 7, 2016, at the age of 90.
