Stefanos Tsitsipas's 17,000 Mykonos beach-side villa complete with pool and sea views

 Posted on September 15, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  893 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

A look inside the jaw-dropping villa tennis star Stefanos Tsitsipas is renting shows celebrities certainly know how to celebrate life post-lockdown. 

The Green tennis ace, 22, who seeded Number 6 in the World in the men's rankings and became famous for defeating his childhood idol Roger Federer at the age of 20, has been sharing glimpse of his break at luxury Villa Lilium on Mykonos for his summer 2020 vacation.  

The magnificent six-bedroom house is located just above the world famous beach of Psarou, and set in a sprawling 5,382 square foot private compound, overlooking the beach and the Aegean Sea. 

The villa offers over 300 square foot of luxury accommodation, with each of the six bedrooms boasting an en-suite bathroom and balconies with sea views - but it comes at an eye-watering price tag of £17,180-per-week.

The villa features a first floor principal bedroom suite with a private relaxation area and ensuite shower room, with direct access to a balcony, overlooking the swimming pool and beyond to the Aegean Sea.

Perfect for sports-loving celebrities and business VIPs alike, it has satellite TV and plasma screens to all principal rooms, air conditioning throughout and WiFi for entertainment, communication and business.

Athens-born Stefanos is the youngest tennis player in the men's top ten and is the highest-ranked Greek tennis player in history.

As a junior player he was ranked number one in the world, with his Greek father a tennis coach, whilst his Russian mother was also a professional tennis player. 

The sportsman said he the destination because he was looking for a 'private, safe and relaxing break from his training schedule and the recent lockdown'.

Roi Deldimou, Manager at Beauchamp Estates (Mykonos) told FEMAIL: 'It was a pleasure to welcome Stefanos to Villa Lilium and Mykonos. 

'As a Greek I am exceedingly proud of his sporting achievements and it has been a pleasure to meet him. Stefanos has chosen to stay in one of the most beautiful villas on the island, which is located in the stunning Agios Lazaros. 

'It is a wonderful location in which to relax, recharge and enjoy the stunning sea views from one of Villa Lilium's many terraces.'

Gary Hersham, Founding Director of Beauchamp Estates added: 'Stefanos is the latest sports star to spend the summer on Mykonos. 

'Each generation discovers this beautiful Greek Island paradise and it is easy to understand why throughout time it has enchanted visitors such Jackie Kennedy, Richard Burton, Brigitte Bardot, Richard Branson and Leonardo DiCaprio.'
