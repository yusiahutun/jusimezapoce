Biography

The actor was born on 12th August 1989 in Udi village, Enugu State in Nigeria. She is the second child in a family of five kids, three males and two females. She has not offered any detailed information about her upbringing and siblings names. Destiny Etiko's parents are also not known to the public.

Destiny Etiko mother was her biggest inspiration in becoming an actress. Her mother was once an actress but later left the industry. Her father, on the other hand, was not entirely in support of Destiny's career choice. He passed away in May 2020.

The actress studied at Enugu for both her primary and secondary school education and obtained First School Leaving Certificate and the West African Senior School Certificate respectively. She relocated from Enugu State to Anambra State to get a university degree.

She enrolled at the Nnamdi Azikiwe University in Awka and graduated with a degree in Theater Arts in 2012. In 2015, she finished her National Youth Service Corps (NYSC).
