15 sites like QVC for online shopping with easy payment plans

 Posted on April 15, 2024  |  7 minutes  |  1483 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Online shopping has come a long way, with several e-commerce stores offering customers the best experience when buying goods online. One of these platforms is QVC, an American home shopping e-commerce store. But are there sites like QVC that you can use for online shopping?

QVC has an Easy Pay payment plan where you can order and receive an item but pay for it in instalments. On top of that, the website does not charge you any interest if you use specific payment methods such as major credit cards or PayPal.

15 sites like QVC for online shopping

Online shopping has gone to the next level and many platforms like QVC have made different strategies to drive more consumers to their sites. According to Tech 21 Century, these are the stores like QVC offering online shopping services:

1. Home Shopping Network (HSN)

Home Shopping Network is one of the best alternatives for QVC. It started as a television show offering home shopping services before transitioning into a full-fledged e-commerce store. The site offers a range of products, from clothing to electronics, cosmetic products, and household goods.

The site offers flexible payment plans through the Flex Pay service, where you can pay for products received in monthly instalments. Furthermore, there is no interest charged on these repayments.

2. Fingerhut

Fingerhut is another top online shopping platform. It offers a wide range of products, from electronics to clothing, jewellery, furniture, power tools, and gardening products. In addition, they have an easy payment service, the Fingerhut FreshStart, and the Fingerhut Advantage program.

Both programs allow you to purchase items on credit and build their limit with timely payments. On top of that, when using the Fingerhut FreshStart program, you can also earn back rewards totalling up to 10% of the purchase price.

3. Overstock

Overstock is famous for offering amazing deals, where you can purchase items from designer brands at a discount. It is also one of the top sites with payment plans like QVC.

It offers a credit card which you can use to purchase items. The company has a lease-to-own payment plan, where you can pay for items in bi-weekly or monthly instalments.

4. MDG

MDG is an e-commerce store where you can purchase electronics, ranging from computers to smartphones, TVs, audio and video equipment, drones, and wearables. However, the store's buy now pay later payment plans make the store stand out. You can access credit of up to $3,000, which you can use to shop for goods in the store.

5. Klarna

Klarna is a shopping gateway and a quasi-online marketplace that allows you to shop from various e-commerce stores. Some of the most popular ones include Bed Bath & Beyond, GameStop, Casetify, Under Armour, Ralph Lauren, etc. The best thing about Klarna is you can use their shop now pay later program to purchase items at any of the stores they work with.

6. BuyDig

BuyDig is another leading e-commerce store dealing with electronics. You can purchase TVs, computers, audio and video equipment, photography equipment, home appliances, and others at amazing prices.

Like QVC, BuyDig also offers easy payment plans for customers. They have a buy now pay later program, where you can purchase items on credit using PayPal Credit or Amazon check out.

7. ShopHQ

ShopHQ (formerly Evine) is an e-commerce store that operates a home shopping TV network, just like QVC. It has a wide selection of goods, from jewellery to watches, household products, electronics, beauty and health products, fashion and clothing items.

The home shopping TV network is not the only similarity ShopHQ has with QVC; they also offer easy payment plans. For example, the store offers a Value Pay program, where you can shop on credit using credit cards, PayPal credit, or the ShopHQ cad. You then pay back the money in flexible instalments, which you can spread over several months.

8. Rent-A-Center

Rent-A-Center is an e-commerce store that deals in furniture and electronics. They are famous for offering a rent-to-own program, where you can purchase items but pay for them in instalments over several months. In addition, the store offers shopping credit of up to $5,000 and flexible payment plans starting from as low as $20 a week.

9. Conn’s Home Plus

Conn's Home Plus (popularly known as Conn's) is an American store chain that operates an e-commerce store. They offer furniture, mattresses, home appliances, and electronics.

Conn’s Home Plus is another one of the top companies similar to QVC when it comes to payment plans. They have an exclusive financing option for customers, where you can pay for items in instalments, spread over several months.

10. Amazon.com

Amazon is one of the largest e-commerce platforms in the world. It is an online marketplace where you can purchase nearly everything from electronics to clothing, automotive parts, cosmetics, home appliances, digital products, etc.

But did you know that Amazon also offers easy payment plans? The leading online store offers a monthly payment plan, where you can shop for specific items and pay for them in instalments (usually spread over 5 months).

11. Ashley HomeStore

Ashely HomeStore is an American-based furniture store with over 200 chains located worldwide. It offers a wide range of furniture items such as beds, mattresses, sofa sets, coffee tables, kitchen furniture, and related items such as home décor, lighting, and rugs.

Ashley’s HomeStore is another one of the top companies like QVC that offer easy payment plans. Through their Ashley Advantage program, you can shop for items and pay for them in flexible monthly instalments, ranging from 6 to 60 months.

12. FlexShopper

FlexShopper is an e-commerce shopping platform where you can shop for various items such as computers, home electronics, furniture, mattresses, smartphones, jewellery, baby and kid products, and more. Their products are curated from top brands, and they often offer several amazing deals.

FlexShopper also offers a lease-to-own payment plan, where you can purchase items on credit and pay for them in weekly instalments. In addition, they offer you a credit limit of up to $2,500, which you can use on any products they offer.

13. Seventh Avenue

Seventh Avenue is another American e-commerce store with a similar payment plan to QVC. They offer a Seventh Avenue Credit program, where you can purchase items on credit and pay for them in flexible monthly instalments starting from $20. The buying credit rises as you continue to make payments on time.

14. Midnight Velvet

Midnight Velvet is an e-commerce fashion store that offers clothing, shoes, jewellery, and fashion accessories. You can also buy home products such as furniture, home décor, kitchen and appliances, and more.

Midnight Velvet also offers an easy monthly payment service. You can shop for items on credit for up to around $1,800 and pay for them in flexible monthly payments (up to 47 instalments starting from $20 to $60.)

15. Ginny’s

Ginny's is an online shopping platform with a diverse catalogue, allowing you to shop for furniture, kitchen appliances, bed & bath items, health and wellness products, and more.

When it comes to payment, Ginny’s offers you a credit plan, where you can shop for items and pay for them later in monthly instalments. Their plan has a credit limit of up to $400. They also have flexible repayments, starting from as low as $10 to $25 per month.
