Stunning Brazilian bombshell 'earns 50 times more with OnlyFans than in volleyball'

Stunning Brazilian bombshell Key Alves has revealed she 'earns 50 times more with OnlyFans' than she does competing as a professional volleyball player. Alves has risen to fame in recent years as a sports star in her home country of Brazil.

This has not been her only route to stardom though, having become an internet sensation on the subscription site OnlyFans. The 22-year-old has amassed immense popularity on Instagram too, where she boasts over five million followers.

Alves has made the most of her internet fame, having reaped the financial benefits which has trumped the earnings she has brought in as a volleyball star.

Discussing her successes she told Globo Esporte: "I'm going into my third year on a professional volleyball team and I consider myself a professional athlete, but things off the court started to grow a lot for me. So I started paying a little more attention to that side career.

"Whether I like it or not, today it's my biggest income. I earn about 50 times more with digital platforms than with volleyball, and more on OnlyFans, because the monthly price of 16 euros is fixed."

Discussing the content she posts on the subscription site, she added: "The photos I post on my OnlyFans page are totally intended for 'light' and professional sessions. No nudity or anything like that. Anyone looking for this on my profile won't find it."

Alves is the most followed professional volleyball player on the planet when it comes to social media, and the Brazilian revealed that worldwide fame is something she has always wanted to achieve. Per Marca, she said: "From a young age, I always told my parents I wanted to be known worldwide.

"More than just a pretty face, more than talent for volleyball, I am also very charismatic with my fans, giving them all my attention. I always like that, making appearances, taking photos, working with marketing.

"So I think that's the secret to reaching the brand I've created now."
