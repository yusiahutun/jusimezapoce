Watch Florence Hendersons Final TV Performance

 Posted on July 3, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  113 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

As news of Florence Henderson’s passing still sinks in for us and for the celebrity sphere, we can take some solace in the fact that everyone’s favorite mid-century mom was fulfilling her best-loved role as long as she could. Just a few days ago, on November 21, Henderson appeared on the season finale of Dancing With the Stars in support of Maureen McCormick — who played her TV daughter Marcia in The Brady Bunch. Henderson, a multitalented performer in her own right, competed in season 11 of the reality dancing show. In the clip above, you can watch Henderson make her own, albeit brief, guest appearance in McCormick’s quick-step performance.
