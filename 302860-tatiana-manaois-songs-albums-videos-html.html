Tatiana Manaois songs, albums, and videos

 Posted on May 29, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1273 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Pop and new age type of music is embraced by most people in the world and Tatiana Manaois has won herself a share of audiences who enjoy such genres of music. Tatiana has grown with the passion for music but it was not until 2010 when she decided to take it more seriously and hopefully make a career out of it. Miss Manaois started by posting cover music videos of profound artists such as Ed Sheeran and Justin Bieber on YouTube. This enabled her to capture the attention of many on social media giving her more followers and subscribers.

Tatiana was born in 1996 in California, America. Being a self-signed artist and song writer, Tatiana Manaois grew her music and produced her first album in 2013 ‘Speak Now or Forever Hold Your Pain' which comprised of songs such as Understand, This Melody, Last Time, Go Crazy, and Difference. She however did not stop posting music video covers of other artists and even her own songs on YouTube and linking up with famous viners to diversify her channel. These combined efforts of the 23 year old have brought her to the lime light and now she is known all over the world for her music and great vocals.
