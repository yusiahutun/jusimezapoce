Arsenal and Chelsea plot stunning moves for Jordon Ibe and Raheem Sterling

 Posted on August 8, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  192 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Sterling, 20, is out of contract at the end of next season and has refused to sign a new deal.

That's alerted the Premier League's top clubs as well as Real Madrid, Barcelona and Bayern Munich.

The thought of losing Sterling - one of the brightest young talents in world football - will stun boss Brendan Rodgers.

And the shock news that Ibe could leave too will send even more warning signs through Anfield.

According to The Sun, the possibility of signing Ibe is being discussed at Arsenal, Chelsea and Manchester City.

Ibe and Sterling are known to be close friends.

They found themselves at the centre of a storm last month after being pictured smoking a sheesha pipe together.

Ibe has been offered a new long-term contract worth £25,000-a-week at Anfield.

That would more than treble his current £7,000-per-week deal but Ibe is stalling over signing it.

The Sun claim Ibe's advisors are demanding DOUBLE the offer on the table.

Sterling, meanwhile, has rejected a £100,000-a-week contract put forward by Liverpool.

He has pulled out of talks and pledged to discuss his future again at the end of the season.
