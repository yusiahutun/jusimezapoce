Model turned actress, Lili Simmons, debuted in the acting career with the web series, Hollywood Is Like High School with Money. Her depiction in the series won the heart of many viewers, who remain keen to know who lives in the heart of this beautiful brunette.

Well, addressing the curiosity, let us tell you the actress has been dating since 2015. But do you know who the lucky guy is?

Lili Simmons' Dating Status Now:

The Banshee actress, Lili Simmons has been in a romantic relationship with the 'Daytime Emmy Awards' winning actor, Tom Pelphrey and their affair reflected through their social media.

Back in August 2015, Lili shared an image on her Instagram where she was spotted enjoying the birthday party thrown by her boyfriend, Tom.

Photo: Lili Simmons's Instagram

Well, Tom too didn't shy off from letting his fans know about his girlfriend. Tom took to Twitter back in the year, 2016, gushing about his love life, indicating Lili as his-

'pretty amazing girlfriend.'

Photo: Tom Pelphrey's Twitter

However, as of now, the two stars prefer to stay silent about their relationship and is not even seen together on the social networking sites. Well, that leads everyone to suspect if they are even together. Well, they are yet to address the fact till this date, keeping it mysterious for everyone else!

Before Tom, Lili dated American actor, Adam Hicks back in 2011. Lilli, 25, hinted about her relationship status through a tweet that read,

"God I love my boyfriend."

Moreover, after the very tweet, Lili tweeted a photo with her then-boyfriend and best friend back in November 2011, adding:

"Chilling in la jolla a couple of days ago at the living room with my best friend and my boyfriend."

Similarly, Lili wished her then-boyfriend Adam Hicks on his Birthday through the tweet back in 2011.

Photo: Lili Simmons' Twitter

They flaunted their relationship in social media, but the ex love-birds separated keeping things low-key, without even disclosing the reason for their separation.

Aside from flaunting her love relationship, Lili flaunted her parents as well. She uploaded the picture of her childhood days on Instagram back in 2017, where little Lili can be spotted with her brother and father. 

Lili, who stands at the height of 1.7 m (5'7"), began her professional career as a model for the Ford Model Agency and continued to work for Roxy and Bebe Stores.

Besides modeling, Lili has starred in various films as well, namely Fat Kid Rules the World (2012), Boone Tomahawk (2015), Dirty Lies (2016) and Bad March (2017).
