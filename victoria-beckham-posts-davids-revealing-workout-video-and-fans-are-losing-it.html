Victoria Beckham posts David's 'revealing' workout video and fans are losing it

 Posted on June 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  376 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Victoria Beckham has been taking one for the team lately as she continues to record ‘thirst trap’ videos of husband David, for all the fans to enjoy. This time, she’s showing him partake in a home workout, and she’s not leaving anything out.

The celebrity couple have been goals ever since they got together in 1997. David told his teammate and best friend Gary Neville he’d marry the Spice Girl one day, and oh, was he right. The pair have now been married for almost 25 years, and have four children together. Now, Victoria’s sharing her husband with all of us.

Victoria Beckham shares ‘revealing’ video of David’s workout

Taking to her 32.3 million happy Instagram followers, the fashion designer posted a video of her husband working out as he stretched in tight shorts.

The former footballer can be seen on the floor stretching back and forth, and Victoria didn’t leave anything out of the shot, zooming in on his ‘backside’.

In the caption, she wrote: “Morning work out with this Love Machine @davidbeckham,” as the song played in the background. Although, we don’t think anyone was paying attention to that.

Judging by the comments, we don’t think anyone was complaining.

“This is a public service,” wrote one.

“On behalf of women everywhere, thank you!” exclaimed another.

A third joked: “I have never been more grateful that she exists than right now.”

“This account is better than OnlyFans,” one joked.

It’s not the first time Victoria’s graced our timelines

It looks like the former Spice Girl is getting into the habit of showing off her husband.

Just a few days before the workout video, the 49-year-old sent the timeline wild as she shared a picture of David fixing their TV in nothing but his boxers.

Although fans couldn’t see his face in the pic, they could see a whole lot more. It looks like Victoria does know exactly what she’s doing, as in the caption, she exclaimed: “Electrician came to fix the TV…. You’re welcome!”

Of course, X, formerly known as Twitter, went wild.

“So unselfish, thank you Victoria,” wrote one grateful fan.

“That woman is flexing and I ain’t mad at her…,” said another.

Honestly, neither are we. Keep them coming!
