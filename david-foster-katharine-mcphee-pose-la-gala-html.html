They confirmed their relationship at the end of last year with a passionate public kiss.

And on Sunday, 68-year-old David Foster and 33-year-old Katharine McPhee both attended the same Grammy viewing party in Los Angeles.

Each of them was photographed solo on the red carpet at the Inaugural Janie's Fund Gala presented by Steven Tyler and Live Nation at Red Studios.

Aglow: This Sunday, 68-year-old David Foster and 33-year-old Katharine McPhee (pictured) both attended the same Grammy viewing party in Los Angeles

Katharine modeled a chic white coat dress, wearing her hair down and accenting her TV star features with an elegant makeup job.

She wore a pewter grey pair of thigh-high stiletto boots, and complemented the footwear with a small clutch in a similar shade.

Meanwhile, David wore a black blazer over a blue and white checked shirt, tucking his top into a pair of faded, torn jeans.

There's David: Each of them was photographed solo on the red carpet at the Inaugural Janie's Fund Gala presented by Steven Tyler and Live Nation at Red Studios

Both of them have had musical careers - David being a business mogul in the industry and Katharine having starred and sung on the TV show Smash.

David has in his time gone through four marriages - to singer B.J. Cook from 1972-1981, to Rebecca Dyer from 1982 to 1986, to songwriter Linda Thompson from 1991 to 2005 and to model-turned-reality-star Yolanda Hadid from 2011 to last year.

His split with Yolanda went public in 2015, but the divorce was not finalized until 2017.

Elegant: Katharine modeled a chic white coat dress, wearing her hair down and accenting her TV star features with makeup

Katharine and her own ex-husband, producer Nick Cokas, tied the not in 2008. In October 2013, McPhee was photographed kissing her married Smash director Michael Morris. 

By May 2014, Cokas and his wife publicly announced their intention to dissolve their union, and the divorce was finalized in February of 2016.

David has sired eight children, whereas Katharine has none at present.
