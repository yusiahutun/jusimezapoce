Ryan Gosling Supports Eva Mendes With His Outfit During Interview

 Posted on March 23, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  355 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Ryan Gosling never misses an opportunity to praise his life partner, Eva Mendes.

While the two stars rarely attend public events together, and purposely keep their relationship and family life out of the Hollywood spotlight, they both regularly find ways to support one another through small, romantic acts.

The Barbie star, for example, wore a necklace featuring an E, for Eva, in Barbie’s signature slanted hot-pink font while doing press for the film last summer. And most recently, he subtly supported Mendes by wearing a shirt featuring the title of her upcoming children’s book during an interview with Hits Radio, for his new film The Fall Guy, posted on YouTube. The cream tee was emblazoned with the title Desi, Mami, and the Never-Ending Worries in black. Gosling—who was joined by costar Emily Blunt for the interview—wore it with white pants, a dangly necklace, and a silver watch.

Hits Radio

Mendes’s picture book, written by the Hitch star and illustrated by Abbey Bryant, will be released on September 17 in both English and Spanish. The book’s cover features an illustration of a mom who resembles Mendes—with her iconic beauty mark—hugging her little daughter.

Gosling and Mendes have two daughters, Esmeralda and Amada.

Mendes has also supported Gosling in his endeavors. Ahead of his performance of “I’m Just Ken” at this year’s Oscars, she was there for her man on rehearsal day, and even shared a clip of herself in his dressing room, and later, a photo of herself wearing the fabulous outfit he wore to perform.

“Always by my man,” she captioned the first post on Instagram. “You took Ken all the way to the Oscar’s, RG. Now come home, we need to put the kids to bed. 💋,” she wrote in the other.
