I Test Clothes for a Living, and I Love These Gwyneth Paltrow-Worn Tees

 Posted on September 3, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  544 words  |  Larita Shotwell

As a fashion editor, I test tons of clothing items, holding them to a very high standard. I often try three new brands I’ve never heard of all in a single outfit, and at the expense of sounding harsh, these labels sometimes blend in with the rest. But there are other times when they stick out and become my new obsession. Goldie Tees falls into the latter category, and for good reason.

If you’ve never heard of Goldie Tees — which also goes by Goldie Lewinter — the sustainable brand prides itself on crafting luxury everyday T-shirts, ranging from long-sleeve options to short-sleeve V-necks. In the words of brand founder Debbie Rudoy, “All you really need to look stunning is the perfect tee and jeans,” and, boy, has Goldie Tees mastered that statement. Just look to the brand's celebrity roster for proof. 

Goldie Tees Organic Jersey Boy Tee

Goldie Tees Ribbed Henley Top


Gwyneth Paltrow was just seen wearing the label’s best-selling Organic Boy Tee, which she layered under a sleek black cardigan and styled with a pencil skirt and Mary Janes. Katie Holmes slipped on the same top with a fringe, suede jacket, while Katherine Schwarzenegger dressed it down with loose-fitting jeans and a woven bucket bag. Lucy Hale went another route, opting for the brand’s ribbed henley long-sleeve top and a paisley-printed jacket, according to the brand. If celebs are wearing them, then you know these tops have to be good.   

Goldie Tees Ribbed Long-Sleeve Tee

While I’m no A-lister, I, too, have heart eyes for Goldie Tees. I own one of the brand’s ribbed long-sleeve shirts and can attest that it’s beyond comfortable. It’s so soft, it feels like butter against my skin, while the stretchy fit allows me to move with freedom. I’ve worn it to industry parties and dinners alike, only to later fall asleep in my shirt — it’s that comfortable.

Being a basic, I have no problem styling the heaven-like top, either. I like to wear mine tucked into a pair of high-waisted jeans and finished off with a pair of heeled booties. Though, I’ve also worn it with sweats, a large puffer jacket, and sneakers. That’s what’s so great about tops from Goldie Tees; they can be worn anywhere and everywhere, whether you’re looking for a casual or elevated outfit thanks to their simple build.       

Goldie Tees Signature Slub Long-Sleeve Tee

Goldie Tees Mutton-Sleeve Knot Tee

While Goldie Tees are basic, they’re far from boring, offering a fun take on a closet essential. Take a look at the brand’s Signature Slub long-sleeve shirt, for example. It looks like a practical top, and it is — except that it features a silky-soft texture that can only be experienced once you slip it on. The brand’s knot tee is the same, offering a chic silhouette alongside a secret pima cotton construction. 

If you’re not sure which Goldie Tee to snag, a quick scan of the brand’s products will definitely point you in the right direction. Or, scroll through the below editor-approved picks for a more seamless selection.
