In 2017, Loose Women host Ruth accidentally let slip that Eamonn was "a steam train" in the bedroom. When talking about how a man's dance moves suggest how he can be in the bedroom, Ruth was quick to share her opinion on her TV star husband.

Ruth told the ITV panel: "Well, if that is the rule my husband is a steam train." Demonstrating his dance moves, she added: "As soon as the music starts, he just goes like a steam train. My husband can be seen to be doing it most Fridays."

Loves Ruth's curves

Ruth also spilled that Eamonn "loves" curves, especially her "boobs, bottom and legs". The presenter told Fabulous magazine: "I still feel sexy, but a lot of that is because my husband makes me feel sexy.

“I don’t walk around all day going, ‘I’m too sexy for my skirt’ but Eamonn loves me and will tell me I’m gorgeous. If you’re tired and you’ve put on weight that’s not conducive to feeling like a sex kitten, but you can have a healthy sex life after the menopause.”

She continued: “Eamonn is my biggest fan and always makes me feel really sexy. When I think I’m at my slimmest, he thinks I’m too thin. He likes me curvy – he loves boobs, bottoms and legs. I’m really lucky because it would be awful to have a man who commented on me piling on the pounds.”

Erotic activity

The couple also admitted to often getting up to 50 Shades of Grey-style antics. On Loose Women, Ruth revealed she has enjoyed "erotica" sessions with Eamonn whilst discussing bondage.

Talking about the racy franchise, Ruth said: "I read the first one and said, 'Who hasn't done a bit of that?' I would call it erotica. I'm not talking whipping and things, but erotica I think, introduced between a couple is good..."

Sex party

The pair also shocked fans after they announced they had once attended a sex party. Eamonn made the revelation in 2016 as he presented ITV's This Morning.

In an interview with White Lines star Angela Griffin, Eamonn said: "Angela, your kids aren't watching - have you ever been to one of those sex parties? We went to one.

"We were thrown out before anything happened. We went to the foreplay. We went to the preamble. We were filming for a series we were doing and my wife said: 'Get out of here before anything starts. Get out.'

The host added: "But you know what the interesting thing was? They were all wearing masks and things and we were filming. The organisers came up to us as we were leaving and said: 'Oh, you can't mention who you saw here.'

"I said: 'Oh, well I didn't see anyone.' They said: 'No, there's someone who works in TV who says you know them. You know them and you have seen them here.'"

Doctors orders

In 2016, Eamonn was in need of a hip replacement and he blamed Ruth for this. The star said it was down to her and her sexual appetite. He confessed to The Mirror: “I’m facing a two-week double hip operation, she wore them out, she’s got to help wear them in.” Ruth then joked: “The doctor says no hanky panky and no strong exercise.”
