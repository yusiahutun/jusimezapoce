Woman Sparks Conversation after Stoping Husband from Paying KSh 840k Bride Price

 Posted on April 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  415 words  |  Larita Shotwell
  • A woman named Jessica Rosinah sparked debate online after sharing details about her marriage
  • In a TikTok video, she revealed that her husband didn't pay the KSh 840k dowry (bride price) her family requested
  • Instead, they decided to get married at the Home Affairs office and invest the money in property

A woman has left netizens intrigued and talking after sharing how her husband didn't pay the KSh 840,00 dowry that was asked of him by her family.

Why woman refused bride price

Jessica Rosinah shared a TikTok video revealing that her uncles had requested the large amount for dowry, but she and her man decided to get married at the Attorney General's office and bought land to build rental rooms.
