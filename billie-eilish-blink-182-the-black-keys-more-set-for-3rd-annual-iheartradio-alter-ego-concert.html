Billie Eilish, Blink-182, The Black Keys &amp; More Set For 3rd Annual iHeartRadio ALTer Ego Concert

 Posted on March 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  279 words  |  Aldo Pusey

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) – Billie Eilish, Blink-182 and The Black Keys have been announced as performers for iHeartRadio’s third annual ALTer EGO concert Presented by Capital One.

Hosted by Woody of iHeartRadio ALT 98.7’s The Woody Show, the one-day event will take place at The Forum in Los Angeles on January 18, 2020, and feature additional performances by The Lumineers, Rex Orange County and Shaed.

“For the third year in a row, iHeartRadio is bringing the biggest names in Alternative and Rock music together for one huge night,” said Lisa Worden, Program Director for ALT 98.7 and Alternative Rock Brand Manager for iHeartMedia. “This is a show everyone will love.”

“I’m pretty sure they meant to call another show to host instead of The Woody Show,” added Woody. “This event, this lineup, is WAY out of our league. That said, it’s going to be incredible. I can’t see how this night could end up any other way!”

Beginning October 28, iHeartRadio will be giving Alt-Rock fans across the country the opportunity to win a trip to Los Angeles to attend iHeartRadio ALTer EGO. The promotion will run across more than 85 Alternative, Rock and Classic Rock 90s iHeartRadio stations.

Additionally, listeners will have access to a limited number of tickets to the event through Alt 98.7’s The LIST On-Sale beginning October 17 at 10:00 a.m. PT through October 18 at 10:00 a.m. PT, or while supplies last. Tickets go on sale to the general public on October 18 at 12:00 p.m. PT.

For those who can’t make it, iHeartRadio ALTer EGO will be livestreamed exclusively on LiveXLive.com with live broadcasts across over 70 Alternative and Rock iHeartRadio stations.
