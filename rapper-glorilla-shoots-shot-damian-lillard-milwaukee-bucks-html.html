American rapper GloRilla shoots her shot with Damian Lillard in now-deleted posts after the Milwauke

 Posted on January 26, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  252 words  |  Larita Shotwell

American rapper GloRilla did not hesitate to shoot her shot with NBA star Damian Lillard over social media this weekend. 

GloRilla posted now-deleted messages on X trying to get the attention of the Milwaukee Bucks point guard, after getting a photo with him at the NBA All-Star game on Sunday night. 

Lillard looks to be single, as he filed for divorce from his wife, Kay’la Hanson, last October. 


'Who n**** dis is? Cause I want him #GetEmGlo,' GloRilla posted on social media. 

'Whoever she is can’t whoop me so I really (don't give a f***)' GloRilla continued. 

GloRilla did not waste an opportunity to shoot her shot with Lillard on Sunday evening on X

Lillard is an eight-time NBA All-Star, winning his first All-Star Game MVP on Sunday night

Lillard's focus appears to be on winning an elusive NBA championship after being traded to the Bucks from the Trail Blazers last September.  

Portland never made it to an NBA Final with Lillard, with its best season coming in 2018-19, with a trip to the Western Conference Finals, where it was swept by the Warriors.  

The Bucks are in third place in the Eastern Conference, easily in a playoff spot. And with a teammate the caliber of Giannis Antetokounmpo, anything is possible in the postseason. 

GloRilla has continued to repost people with the phrase 'Yeah Glo' since the messages about Lillard were posted. 

GloRilla has a Grammy nomination and her most-successful song, 'Tomorrow', reached a peak of No 9 on the US Billboard charts.
