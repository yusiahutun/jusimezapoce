Joseph Quinn On Stranger Things

Cult famous TV show Stranger Things season 4 premiered on 27 May 2022. One of the new faces we see on the show is British actor Joseph Quinn who plays Eddie Munson. Eddie is an 80s metalhead and master of Hawkins High’s premier Dungeons & Dragons group, the Hellfire Club.

While he ran the Hellfire Club in the show, he is yet to master the game himself in real life. Joseph told Entertainment Weekly, “I played my first game in L.A. when we were doing some press. I bought a book to try and understand what Dungeons & Dragons was about, and it didn’t agree with me. Though, I knew that wasn’t going to be my way in, as it were”.

Joseph shared with Entertainment Weekly, that his way into his character was through music. He added, “I listened to a lot of heavy metal and that was my… God, it’s impossible to not sound pretentious when you say it, but, yeah, that was my way in”.

The actor knows how to play guitar. He adored Gibson SG after watching Jack Black’s movie School of Rock. He opened up, “I’ve played since I was 7, and I’ve had huge breaks,” he admits. “I wouldn’t consider myself a brilliant guitarist, but I can play it. That was very lucky because those scripts came out, I think, at some point in the pandemic. I did start practicing pretty furiously”.

“He is both a metalhead and a dungeon master, a nerd, and punk,” said the show’s creators.

Joseph Quinn Net Worth

From his acting career, Joseph Quinn has already amassed $300 thousand in net worth.

Joseph Quinn has been performing since he was a small child. In nursery school, he performed a play. He was a drama scholar in high school, so he received a discount on my tuition on the condition that he pursue acting. In his final year of the sixth form, he wrote a play, which they performed in Edinburgh.

In the same year, the British actor was accepted into the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art (LAMDA). Joseph received his LAMDA Diploma in 2015. He is the sort who believes that actors are created rather than born. Furthermore, because of his time in drama school, he began to take acting seriously.

Curtis Brown was Joseph’s agent while he was at LAMDA. Joseph had previously attended Emanuel School, where he began in 2007.

Joseph honed his acting skills in the theatre, as do most performers. He appeared in plays such as Henry IV Part 1 (as Prince Hal), Titus Andronicus (as the main character), and The Seagull (as Konstantin) at LAMDA.

In addition, Joseph played Maurice in the TV drama Deathwatch at Print Room. He also played Dean in the Royal Court Theatre production of Wish List. In addition, he played Luke in the National Theatre production of Mosquitoes.

Postcode, Dickensian, Timewasters, Game of Thrones, Howards End, Les Miserable, Catherine the Great, Small Axe, Lethal White, and now the Stranger Things are some of his notable on-screen works to date.

In addition, the actor appeared in films such as Makeup and Overlord. His track record indicates that he has had success with period drama. Joseph considers sharing a stage with Olivia Colman at The National to be the proudest achievement of his acting career.

Joseph Quinn Family

Joseph Quinn was born in South London. To this date, his parents and siblings are away from the limelight. So, we are yet to learn about his family life. But, while speaking with Joseph shared about his youthful times and having older friends which he relates to being on Stranger Things.
