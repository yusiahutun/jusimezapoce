Jennifer Lawrence Filming Nude Scenes &quot;Red Sparrow&quot;

 Posted on July 1, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  346 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

In one of her most challenging roles to date, Jennifer Lawrence hits the big screen this Thursday in the much-anticipated thriller Red Sparrow.

The actress stepped out of her comfort zone as for the film, which revolves around themes of sex, violence and psychological manipulation and involved filming many scenes fully nude. Lawrence says her decision to take on the role was not easy, but ultimately left her feeling a strong sense of female empowerment.

In a recent interview with Sunrise, the Oscar-winner opened up about her decision to film fully naked, an especially sensitive prospect to Lawrence following her traumatic nude hacking scandal back in 2014.

“I had to really think about it before I even said yes to the movie, obviously," J.Law explained. "Because I couldn’t say yes to the movie and then be like, ‘But can we not use those scenes?’"

It was her pull towards the gripping Red Sparrow script that ultimately urged Lawrence to agree to play the lead role of Russian spy, Dominika Egorova, and face the added obstacle of being fully naked in front of the cameras. Luckily, Lawrence credited the film's supportive cast and crew for making those emotionally-fueled scenes a lot easier for her.

“Everybody went out of their way to make sure that I felt comfortable," Lawrence explained. "And not just comfortable, they made me feel like I had clothes on," she continued in fun, "Almost to a dangerous point where I’d just be like talking and someone’s like, ‘Do you want to put on a robe?’"

Upon self-reflection, Lawrence told 60 Minutes that in a way, stripping down was a way for her to take back what had been taken from her in her privacy scandal years ago.

"I realize that there's a difference between consent and not," J.Law explained. "I showed up for the first day, and I did it, and I felt empowered. I feel like something that was taken from me, I got back, and I'm using it in my art."

Red Sparrow opens in theaters nationwide this Friday, March 2nd.
